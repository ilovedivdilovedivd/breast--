Muscle flex deformity: I have 520cc silicone implants placed sub-muscularly.
Animation Deformity
Jan 16, 2018
You have animation deformity from having breast implants under the muscle.  This is normal and common and more exaggerated early.  As your muscle relaxes and accommodates the large breast implants, this deformity will soften.  It can take a year or longer. If it persists or is exaggerated, revision surgery may be needed. Some amount of this small imperfection must be tolerated.Best Wishes,Nana Mizuguchi, MD
