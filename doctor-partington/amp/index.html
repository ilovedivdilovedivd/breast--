Statement: Dr. Marshall T. Partington is a double board-certified plastic surgeon in Kirkland, Washington. With more than 30 years in practice, Dr. Partington has helped thousands of patients in the greater Seattle area and around the world realize their aesthetic goals. He specializes in breast enhancement, body contouring procedures like S.A.F.E. liposuction and drainless tummy tucks, and complete mommy makeovers.

Dr. Partington’s state-of-the-art practice, Partington Plastic Surgery & Laser Center, is devoted to enhancing the lives of his patients. “I find great joy in helping people look and feel their best at any age,” he says. “I view each patient’s story as a new journey, with a very specific objective and potential result.” He and his staff provide safe and comfortable care, with sincere dedication to helping his patients look and feel their best with natural-looking results.

Dr. Partington obtained his medical degree from Cornell University Medical College, where he was named president of his class. He went on to complete a seven-year general surgery residency at the University of California, Los Angeles. There, he was appointed as a research fellow for the department of cardiothoracic surgery.

He continued his advanced training with a plastic surgery residency at New York University and a microsurgery fellowship at the Buncke Clinic Davies Medical Center in San Francisco.

Before starting his private practice, Dr. Partington held the roles of director of microsurgery at the University of Pennsylvania Medical Center and chief of plastic surgery at the Everett Clinic in Washington state.

Dr. Partington is board certified by both the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is a member of various medical societies, including the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Society of Plastic Surgery, and the Northwest Society of Plastic Surgeons.

He has been the recipient of numerous awards and honors, including Kirkland’s Best Plastic Surgeon, Top Doctor in Seattle Met magazine, and Best of Western Washington’s Best Plastic Surgeon for six consecutive years.

When he’s not helping patients at his Seattle-area private practice, Dr. Partington volunteers and serves as a committee member for the nonprofit organization ReSurge International, which provides free plastic and reconstructive surgery to patients worldwide with facial anomalies and injuries.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Dr. Partington and his colleagues raised funds to provide PPE for the medical unit and the community of the West African city of Bamako.
