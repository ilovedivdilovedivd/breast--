Will a bra line back lift remove skin that was missed during a tummy tuck?
Back lift
Nov 20, 2023
A back lift has nothing to do with an extended abdominoplasty. They are two different operations that treat two different areas. An extended abdominal plasty or trunk lift works below the waist and a back lift works above the waist.

A back lift is offered combined with a breast lift. I prefer them secondary to the tummy because they are pulling in different directions.

After a month or so, without an implant, it can be replaced.
