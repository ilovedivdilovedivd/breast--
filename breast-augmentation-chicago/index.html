The Best Breast Implants In Chicago, IL

Breast augmentation in Chicago, IL is a simple plastic surgery, the essence of which is to install the implant under the breast or under the pectoralis major muscle. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, hospitalization takes only two days, and a week later you can go to work.

The success of the breast enlargement depends on three factors: proper selection of implants, medical skills the surgeon during the bust enlargement, the compliance with all recommendations of the doctor in the postoperative period.

One of the main tasks of a specialist is define what woman expects the from augmentation mammoplasty and, according to it needs, surgeon can offer the best option of the implant, its mode of installation and the type of surgical approach.

Breast Augmentation Chicago Includes Insertion Of A Silicone Bag (prosthesis) Under The Breast (submammary) Or Under The Breast And Chest Muscle

The shape of the implants are of two types: round and anatomical.

The Americans prefer the round shape of the breast – like an apple, equally convex top and bottom. Europeans prefer another form – anatomical: smoothed on the top pole and convex on the bottom pole.

If you install implants of round shape a few months later after the bust enhancement becomes evident that the breast looks unnatural. While anatomically shaped implants give the illusion of natural breasts. Only a specialist can determine that mammoplasty was performed. The price of implants varies greatly, depending on the manufacturer, from 600 to 3000 US dollars for a pair. We use implants, which in our opinion, the most optimal in terms of price and quality.

Different materials may be used for breast enlargement, but preference is given to silicone implants. They’re absolutely safe. The problems that have been with them 15-20 years ago are long gone. Formerly, indeed, it was frequent complications after mammoplasty due to unfinished fabrication technology implants.

Breast Augmentation Cosmetic Surgery In Chicago, IL Involves Adding Something To The Breast To Create The Volume And Shape

Currently, implants are safe for human health. Those complications that may arise now, usually associated with non-compliance by patients the physician requirements in the postoperative period.

Modern implants are absolutely safe: they are very textured, in a three-layer shell that eliminates the rupture or leak silicone implant. The implant is filled with gel, which in consistency like a jelly, it does not penetrate through the shell into the tissue.

Implants are guaranteed for life from producer. The patient is entitled to a free replacement of the implant. Breast implant is installed through an incision measuring about 4 cm, which is located either under the breast, around the areola-nipple or under the armpit. Each method has its pros and cons.

Breast Implant Operation In Chicago, Illinois Is Performed Alone Or In Combination With Other Available Breast Enhancement Procedures

Inframammary access – an incision in anatomical crease under the breast. This is the safest and easiest way to install. This access provides a direct path for the implant, and requires a small amount of time for the breast enlargement procedure. The disadvantage of this type of access is the presence of visible scarring.

Periareolar approach – an incision is along the border of the pigmented portion of the nipple. Such access leaves little scarring. Scarring will not remain if correctly chosen the size of the implant and the operation was performed correctly. This method is by far the most frequently used in mammoplasty.

Transaxillary access – an incision made to the armpit. This access allows avoiding scar on the breast after the augmentation mammoplasty. If properly an access made: on the natural crease, it will be invisible. Transaxillary emplacement can be performed bluntly or with an endoscope. The disadvantage of access at carrying out breast augmentation in Chicago that forming a cavity for placement of the implant occurs almost blindfold, increasing the risk of complications.
