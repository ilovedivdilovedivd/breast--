Hardening or softening of the implant. Is something going on with the implant?
My advice to you
Aug 24, 2021
Hardening of the breast with implants inserted can sometimes be a sign of capsular contracture. The best recommendation is to see your plastic surgeon for an in person assessment and also a breast ultrasound to diagnose if there are any complications arising from your breast implants.
Upper body weight lifting after silicone under the muscle implants?
Six Months
Aug 19, 2021
I typically advise waiting six months until performing any chest exercises. Please discuss this with your plastic surgeon to ensure you are following their guidelines following your breast augmentation surgery.
How to prevent wound dehiscence?
Tips
Aug 19, 2021
Keeping your arms by your side (no lifting arms above shoulder height) for at least the first three to four weeks, as this will allow your incisions time to heal and prevent any wound breakdowns. Also ensuring that you don’t exercise, try to eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of fluids. Smoking and recreational drugs can affect wound healing, it is best to avoid these pre and post surgery. Please consult with your plastic surgeon for all of their specific post operative instructions.
My nose is crooked 8 months post-op. Is it realistic to believe my nose will beco
Potential swelling
Aug 19, 2021
Swelling after a Rhinoplasty can last up to twelve months post operatively. Swelling can cause an asymmetrical appearance as swelling can reduce differently from side to side. I would recommend to discuss your concerns of asymmetry with your plastic surgeon as they will be able to advise if this is due to swelling.
How long do I have to wait to get an explant surgery?
Too early to tell
Aug 19, 2021
It is very common for patients not to like their physical appearance in the immediate post operative period after surgery. It can take approximately three to six months for visible results including the shape and size of your breasts, as the swelling needs to reduce and the breast tissue needs to settle around the implant. I would recommend allowing your implants to settle and discuss your concerns with your plastic surgeon.
