Do i need a breast lift or implants?

Implants and/or lift

I would recommend that you have a lift only if you’re comfortable with the size of your breasts. Otherwise I would consider doing an augmentation and a lift.

The location of your incision would depend ultimately on the size of implant that you have.

The bigger the implants, the less skin removal, hence the possibility of not having a vertical scar or horizontal scar on the breast.

I would definitely recommend getting another consultation with a board-certified plastic surgeon. (Young R. Cho, MD, PhD, Houston Plastic Surgeon)

Do I need a Breast Lift or will an implants be okay?

Do i need a breast lift or implants photo

I my opinion, you will be sadly disappointed with just a breast augmentation. The procedure indicated in you is a full breast lift with the insertion of breast implants. If you aren’t ready accept the scars and are tight with your money, then just wait.There are surgeons who prefer to do one or the other procedure first and then come back and to the other. If you have the breast lift done first and then have the augmentation, you will still have to deal with the scars of the lift. You would then come back months later and have the breast augmentation. I you do the opposite and have the augmentation done first, you will be very disillusioned with the results and then have to wait to do the lift.Understand that there are advantages of doing both at the same time… less costly, one anesthesia, one recovery. Think about it. In any case, seek out a second opinion. (Jose Perez-Gurri, MD, FACS, Miami Plastic Surgeon)
