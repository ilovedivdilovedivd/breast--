How To Pick a Plastic Surgeon For Breast Augmentation?

Finding the “best” breast augmentation plastic surgeon for you

There are several criteria for the breast augmentation plastic surgeon for you. First, he or she must be certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery which means that not only have they completed full training in plastic surgery but have also passed a rigorous testing of their knowledge.

Second, you must feel comfortable with your surgeon and be able to explain your goals and understand how your surgeon is planning to me them. You may need to have consultations with several surgeons in your area until you find one you can trust. Word of mouth is a good way to find out about surgeons in your area so ask around.

And you can ask your regular doctor for recommendations too. (Joseph Serota, MD, Aurora Plastic Surgeon)

Choose a board-certified breast augmentation plastic surgeon.

Plastic surgery board certification is an excellent place to start your search. There are plenty of “cosmetic surgeons” but they are not Plastic Surgeons and so their training and motives are questionable. There is no specific “best breast augmentation surgeon” but rather many excellent surgeons across the country.

After certification, be sure to visit several offices in the area, ask to see before and after pictures, and ask if you may talk to any prior patients. Ask any question about the procedure and your surgeon’s answers should make you feel comfortable or else find another doctor. You will probably find the breast augmentation plastic surgeon that you are looking for by doing this. (Douglas S. Steinbrech, MD, New York Plastic Surgeon)

The Best Breast Surgeon

There are many excellent plastic surgeons out there – but there are also many “want to be” cosmetic surgeons. When you are trying to find the best breast surgeon for you, first make sure your surgeon is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. With this certification, you can be certain that they are a fully trained plastic surgeonand qualified to perform breast augmentation. The next step is to review their society affiliations – surgeons who are members of recognized aesthetic societies (the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery) devote a significant portion of their practice to aesthetic surgery, and are recognized by their peer board certified plastic surgeons as being qualified to perform cosmetic surgery.

The final step is personal taste – even among this group of expert surgeons, personal artistry plays a role in the results that a surgeon achieves. You should spend time reviewing the before and after galleries of the surgeons you are considering, and see if the results match your taste.

Everyone has different anatomy, but the small decisions that surgeons make during an operation do have an impact on the final result. Since you cannot control the way someone thinks, it is best to find someone who thinks the same way that you do. In my mind, this is the best way to end up with a result that will make you happy. (Ricardo A. Meade, MD, Orange County Plastic Surgeon)
