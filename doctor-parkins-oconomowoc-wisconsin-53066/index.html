May 19, 2019
The answer to this depends on what you think looks natural.  Remember that as the band size goes up (32,34,36, etc) the volume of the cup also gets larger.  This means that a “c-cup” is more actual breast tissue for a 36C vs a 34C.  A 36 B is the same volume of breast tissue as 34 C.  Most people will say anything between a B and C looks natural but some people think a D looks natural.  Visit a board certified plastic surgeon and she will be able to guide you.  Best wishes!
