Selecting the shape and the surface of implants in augmentation

The surface of the implant can be smooth or fluted (textured). The textured implants reduces the degree of development of the so-called capsular contracture which is a deformation of the visual forms of breast due to the formation the capsule around implant consisting of elements of fibrous tissue (fibrous tissue is formed almost always as a protective reaction of the organism around a foreign body that has entered the the organism).
