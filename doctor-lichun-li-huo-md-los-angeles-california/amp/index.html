How often and how deep would I have to do Skinpen microneedling?
Acne scars
Feb 9, 2020
Hi,Microneedling to treat acne scars would require 4-6 sessions, 4 to 6 weeks apart.  The depth can be up to 2.5mm.  However, varying depths may be needed depending on your response and area of face.  I would actually also recommend chemical peels which are typically more economical than lasers/microneedling to help treat these acne scars, a daily skincare regimen with products that contain anti-oxidants, retinol, or glycolic acid. Best,Dr. Huo
How long after microneedling treatment (.5mm) can I use *chemical* sunscreen?
Sunscreen
Oct 29, 2019
Hi, I do not recommend putting on sunscreen for the first 24 hours after microneedling.  Wear a hat, and avoid the sun.  Use what is directed.  After the 24 hours, I’d recommend mineral sunscreen (Avene makes a great one that’s reasonably priced).  After 3 days, you can go back to using your chemical sunscreen.Best,Dr. Huo
Can I do Thermalift after PicoSure laser facial?
PicoSure
Oct 29, 2019
Hi,I actually sometimes combine both procedures (Thermage/thermismooth and Pico) in one appointment.  I would do the RF thermi procedure first, wait about 45 minutes (perfect time to apply numbing cream in preparation for the PicoSure laser) then perform PicoSure.  Now, if you’ve already had PicoSure laser, I’d recommend waiting for about 2 weeks before Thermalift.  Of course, you should discuss with the medical provider with whom you’ll be undergoing treatment.  Best,Dr. Huo

What’s a safe percentage TCA peel to do for under eyes and eye lids, if at all possible?
Peels
Oct 29, 2019
HI,If your concern is hooded eyelids and saggy lower eyes, your best option may not be chemical peels.   I would not advise doing TCA peels on your own.  Glytone makes a eye/lip peel that is available to medical professionals which contains TCA.  Please seek the consultation of a professional aesthetic/cosmetic provider and see what options are presented to you to address your concerns.Best,Dr. Huo
Can you get ThermiVa after a trim labiaplasty? Will it help smooth the scars?
ThermiVa
Oct 29, 2019
HI,You can have thermiva after vulvar surgery, however, I’d wait until 3 months has elapsed.  There’s currently no recommendation/evidence that ThermiVa will help to dissolve/resolve scar tissue. If you have vulvar pain, please see a vulva specialist to figure out the etiology before you spend a lot of time, energy and money trying out various procedures.Best,Dr. Huo
