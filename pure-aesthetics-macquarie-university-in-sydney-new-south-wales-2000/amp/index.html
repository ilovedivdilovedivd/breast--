A recognized leader in the field of cosmetic plastic surgery, Dr. Merten trained at the esteemed Royal Australian College of Surgeons. He specializes in reconstructive and cosmetic plastic surgery.

At his Sydney practice, Pure Aesthetics, Dr. Merten specializes in all aspects of body work, including cosmetic breast enhancement surgery and body-contouring procedures such as tummy tucks and body lifts following major weight loss.

After receiving his medical degree from Sydney University, Dr. Merten completed fellowships in cosmetic surgery at Melbourne Institute of Plastic Surgery and Monash University in Australia, and in breast reconstruction at the University of British Columbia, Canada.

Is it possible to just raise the nipples without a breast lift?
Nipple Lifting
May 13, 2019
Unfortunately there is no way to raise the nipples without removing skin above them, although placing a breast implant can do this if your breasts aren't too droopy. If it is only a small amount of lift needed, then a half-circle scar around the upper border of the areolar may be enough. If more lift is required, a full-circle may be needed. These scars usually heal very well. Regards, Dr Steve Merten, Sydney
