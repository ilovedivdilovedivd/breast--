Cengizhan Ekizceli, MD

Name: Cengizhan Ekizceli, MD
Statement: Dr. Cengizhan Ekizceli, MD is a plastic surgeon in Istanbul, Turkey. He has spent 12 years in practice performing a wide range of aesthetic and reconstructive surgeries, including Forehead Reduction, liposuction, tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), Facelift, breast augmentation and eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty), as well as non-surgical procedures, such as Botox and dermal fillers.

Many of Dr. Ekizceli’s patients seek out his expertise as a facial plastic surgeon. Facelifts and rhinoplasty (nose jobs) are two of his most popular procedures, alongside facial rejuvenation with fat injections. Taken mostly from the body’s own belly, hips, and thighs, fat injections can lift the eyebrows, balance the chin and forehead, fill the nasolabial folds, and increase volume in the lips, cheekbones, and temples, giving the face a fresher, younger look.

Dr. Ekizceli is also an accomplished breast surgeon who sees patients for breast augmentation, breast lifts, breast reduction, and gynecomastia. Sometimes, breast lift surgeries are performed alongside breast augmentation or reduction to improve size, shape, and volume simultaneously. Throughout his career, Dr. Ekizceli has found that breast reduction is one of the most rewarding surgeries to perform because patients, free from the painful extra weight of heavy breasts, appreciate the results so much.

Born in Çankırı, Dr. Ekizceli received his medical degree from Istanbul University Cerrahpaşa Faculty of Medicine where he remained to complete his specialty training in plastic aesthetic and reconstructive surgery. Dr. Ekizceli served his compulsory military service at Istanbul’s Kanuni Sultan Süleyman Training and Research Hospital before starting his practice at Medicine Hospital in 2019.
