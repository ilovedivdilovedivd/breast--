Is it safe to have TT with muscle repair & lipo along with breast lift & augmentation?
Achieving results with safety
Aug 10, 2020
These procedure can be done safely together as long as there is adequate risk stratification. Most of these combination procedures, notably tummy tuck, are often placed on blood thinners as part of the procedure.
Can I get 360 Lipo, tummy tuck, and transfer the fat to my breast with a lift?
Helping achieve your goals
Aug 10, 2020
Yes, all these can be done at once. From your photographs, you are relatively lean so there is likely less fat available for transfer. Similarly, with a lift, there is only so much space to be able to graft o breasts. You need a second stage of fat transfer depending on the volume you desire.

Managing Weight Loss
Aug 10, 2020
Stable weight (regardless of how it is obtained) is more important to overall shape. I prefer patients to have stable weight for at least 6 months before body contouring surgery. If you are still anticipating more weight loss beyond 5-10 lbs, I would wait. It is a large investment and you want to be in optimal shape for the best possible results.

What's the procedure with a mommy makeover?
Traditional vs. Non-Traditional
Aug 10, 2020
Butt lift is not traditionally included in a standard mommy makeover since it is not something that necessarily changes with pregnancy. Breast intervention (lift, implant, etc.) is part of a traditional mommy makeover.

Can plastic surgeons fix incisional hernias (bulge) and revise TT (small bulge after hysterectomy) at
Occasionally Yes
Jun 22, 2020
Incisional hernia “can” be done together if small enough and without need for intra-abdominal mesh. Traditionally, larger hernias are done in conjunction with a general surgeon and a plastic surgeon. Discussing with a board certified plastic surgeon and examining the area with films/CT scans can help determine whether you are a good candidate for a combination procedure.

Weight is stable 8 months after vertical sleeve gastrectomy. Can I get a tummy tuck and BBL?
Best to wait at least 18 months
Jun 22, 2020
After massive weight loss surgery, we usually recommend 18 months after surgery with 6 months of stable weight within ideal +/- 5 lbs. However, surgery can be done before the 18 month marker, but the outcome is less predictable as the body is still acclimating to the changes of massive weight loss.
