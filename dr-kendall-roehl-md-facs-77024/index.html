When can I sleep on my stomach after breast implants?
How to Sleep Correctly With Breast Implants
Aug 23, 2021
Hello! I advise my patients not to lie on their stomachs for the first few weeks after breast implant surgery. For added comfort, you can place a pillow on your side to maintain your position. You can also sleep at a reclined angle with the support of pillows behind you to reduce the pressure on your implants so they can heal faster.
Can You Sleep on Your Stomach with Breast Implants?
Sleeping on Stomach After Breast Augmentation
Aug 23, 2021
Hi! I typically advise my patients not to sleep on their stomachs after breast augmentation surgery for as long as possible until they are fully recovered. The reason for this is to give the scar capsule enough time to form around the implant and to eliminate the risk of deformity that can be caused by the pressure of sleeping on the implants. Instead, I suggest that my patients sleep on their side with a pillow behind and in front of them so they do not roll over in the middle of the night…
What’s the difference between a breast lift and breast argumentation?
Difference Between Breast Lift and Breast Augmentation
Aug 22, 2021
Hi! A breast lift surgically reshapes the breasts, tightens sagging or loose skin, and lifts the breasts to a more youthful position. Patients who undergo a breast lift usually suffer from drooping breasts, whether due to aging or pregnancy, and would like to restore its appearance. Breast augmentation involves the use of implants to improve the shape of the breasts. Breast augmentation can also help correct pronounced asymmetry in patients’ breasts.
