How soon after cheek fillers can I get facial fat transferred to the cheeks?
When can I get fat grafts to my cheeks if I just had 4 syringes of filler placed?
Apr 21, 2019
The obvious question is why would you need fat grafts in your cheeks if you just had 4 syringes placed?  Think about more filler or dissolving filler if there are irregularities or finding another injector.  Having fat placed with filler in is not a problem for chemistry - they will not react with another.  How would a surgeon even know where or how much fat to put in?  Fat grating ideally would be done with no filler in for the best result from fat grafting.Dr. Zenn
Hard swollen lump 2 months post facial fat transfer, what can I do for this lump?
Firm red area in the cheeks 4 months after fat grafting
Apr 21, 2019
It sounds like you are being treated for an infection and this is appropriate.  This may be related to surgery near the salivary glands in the cheek now causing infection.  Your surgeon will aspirate the area with a needle to be sure there is no abscess (trapped infection).  If so, the area will need to be drained.  Infections around fat grafts can lead to lower take rates and lumps requiring further treatment, like steroid injection, but not with an active infection.Dr. Zenn
Do I need to dissolve filler prior to having a fat transfer to my temples?
Should I have fillers put in my temples if I am having fat grafting in 5 months?
Apr 21, 2019
No.  Try to avoid fillers in the areas where fat grafting will be performed.  It's hard enough to get the right amounts of fat grafts to survive and look good.  It is also hard to fully dissolve all of the filler.  Why set yourself up for disappointment or a second round of fat grafting.  Save your money and wait a few months.Dr. Zenn
How can I reduce the amount of fat after facial fat graft?
How can I reduce the fat grafts in my forehead 2 weeks after surgery?
Apr 21, 2019
Two weeks is simply too early to tell how things will look.  2 months can be night and day so hang in there.  Hopefully you will be happy with the cheeks and smile lines when the swelling resolves there too.Dr. Zenn

How soon after fave fat transfer can you workout and go into a sauna?
How soon for exercise and saunas after facial fat grafting.
Apr 21, 2019
Two weeks is about right.  No open areas, no risk of bleeding.  Expect swelling in the surgery areas the first few months of exercise.Dr. Zenn
Can fat injected into my lips migrate to my philtrum?
Fat migration from the lip to the philtrum?
Apr 21, 2019
When fat is placed in the lip, it becomes trapped and does not move.  It is more likely your surgeon placed some in there as an aesthetic maneuver.  I find it easier to make separate stab sites in the mid-cheek to address the cheek.  I also address the lips from separate stab sites in the skin outside the corners.  It is so small that one cannot see the scars.  I can understand why they would be afraid of scarring, just not my experience.Dr. Zenn
