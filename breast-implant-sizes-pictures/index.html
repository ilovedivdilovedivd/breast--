Implant size choice with breast implant sizes pictures

Selection of the implant size is one of the most anxiety provoking aspects of breast augmentation. There are many implants to choose from, and many women and surgeons make the mistake of using volume or cc’s as the primary focus of their selection. In my experience, a more 3 dimensional approach will give a more natural and predictable result. The first measurement that needs to be taken is the base width of your breast. The implant selected should match the footprint of your breast to avoid a gap between the breasts. The projection could then be selected based on how full you wish to look. In this way, the volume can be backed into. Based on your desire to look full, I would suggest a moderate plus profile implant. You can then determine which one based on your breast width. Your plastic surgeon and breast implant sizes pictures can help you with this selection. (Jeffrey Rockmore, MD, Albany Plastic Surgeon)

Pre-operative implant sizing: how to get the right size?

There is no absolute way to predict the perfect size for each person. In general, a narrow chest will add a cup size for every 150-200 ccs, a moderate width 175-225, and a wide chest about 200-250 ccs of volume.

Most patients choose a final volume between 350 and 500 ccs. Implants come in approximately 30-50 ccs increments, ie 390 or 420 ccs. 30 cc is nearly 1 ounce ie 1/8th of a cup in a measuring cup. This amount spread over the entire breast is very small. Most that want a change in size after initial augmentation usually change by adding 150-200cc, or occasionally decreasing by that amount.The most important aspect of sizing is to narrow down the range of implants to a size that looks good for you.

To do this we use a combination of sizing techniques including a computer simulation of your chest, and using specifically shaped implants to mold to your chest. With these we hope to narrow your desired range of implants to get a high degree of satisfaction with the size you choose.By finding a good implant range for you, we hope to decrease the 30% of patients that have another implant surgery to change their undesired size down to 5-10%. Hopefully, this is an added value we can provide our patients. (Scott Haupt, MD, Salt Lake City Plastic Surgeon)

How to choose the right breast implant size?

The implant size is an important point for many patients asking for breast augmentation, but in reality the decision should be make based on your chest wall dimensions, your skin and soft tissue quality. These measurements and evaluations are necessary to determine what size works for you, both in short term and log term.

This evaluation also determine what kind of implant to use, saline, silicone or a form-stable implant. I usually go through this with the patients and then give them a safe range to choose from. You have to bear in mind that long term stability of the results are the more important thing to consider when undergoing a breast augmentation. (Farhad Rafizadeh, MD, Morristown Plastic Surgeon)

375 or 400 cc breast implants will work for you.

Your plastic surgeon can help you decide on breast implant size by taking biometric measurements of your breast and chest wall and based on these measurements and optimal implant size for your body can be determined. However your choice of size based on using the breast implant sizing system and placing various sizes into your bra must also be taken into account.

Moderate plus breast implants look more natural than a high profile implants. In the end I would follow your plastic surgeons recommendations. (Brooke R. Seckel, MD, FACS, Boston Plastic Surgeon)
