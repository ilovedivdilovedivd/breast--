Does pressing breast increase its size?

The BRAVA System for increasing breast size

We offer this system at my practice as an alternative to surgery as a form of natural breast enhancement, or in conjuncture with fat transfer to the breasts.

It has proven itself successful for increasing patients by about half to one cup size when worn alone and for the recommended amount of time.

My patients will wear the device for 10 hours each day (usually in the evening or overnight hours) for anywhere from 4-8+ weeks depending on their results and whether they plan on having fat transfered into their breasts following the use of their BRAVA device.

Does pressing breast increase its size picture

While the initial size change is caused by swelling, the prolonged use of the BRAVA will actually grow new breast tissue.

I tell my patients that once they have reached their desired size, they need to continue wearing the device for the period of time that it took to get them to that cup size.

For example, if you are happy with the results after 4 weeks of wear, you need to wear the device for an additional 4 weeks to maintain that cup size. (Tanya Aya Atagi, MD, Denver Plastic Surgeon)

Increasing breast size without implants

Does pressing breast increase its size quesion

External negative pressure (“sucking”) with a device called BRAVA can increase the size of the breasts at least temporarily, sometimes permanently. This device is usually used in preparation for Autologous Fat Transfer (AFT) to the breasts.

This AFT surgery involves liposuctioning of unwanted fat from another part of the body and then grafts this fat into the breasts. If this is something that sparks your interest, make an appointment with your local plastic surgeon to see if this is a procedure that is offered in his/her practice. (Gregory Park, MD, San Diego Plastic Surgeon)
