Placement of the implant:

Retromammary space is used for implants install almost 80% of cases and is most optimal for breast augmentation. This the implant placement does not violate the integrity of the breast, which eliminates the possibility of pathology. After the augmentation mammoplasty the woman can have children and breastfeed without fear for their health.

Possible complications

After breast augmentation may develop any general surgical complications (abscess wounds, bleeding, thrombosis, thromboembolism, formation of keloid scars, etc..), and the following complications typical for this operation:

– Hematoma (accumulation of blood in the postoperative cavity). A thorough hemostasis during breast surgery, drainage and proper behavior of the patient in the postoperative period can help avoid complications.

– Inflammatory processes to form a liquid in the cavity around implants (including aseptic nature). Observing the rules of sterility and antiseptics, as well as adhering to the rules of behavior in the postoperative period can prevent the development of this complication.

– The formation of capsular contracture. This type of complication is specific for augmentation mammoplasty in Columbus OH. After some time, the body is isolating the foreign object and covers the implant of a fibrous capsule, compressing and deforming it.
