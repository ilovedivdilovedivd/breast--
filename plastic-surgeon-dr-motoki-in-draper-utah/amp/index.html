Is it possible to have a TUBA explant done?
Breast Implant Removal After TUBA
Jan 15, 2018
I have never done a TUBA so you should ask the original surgeon if breast implant removal is possible through the umbilicus. I think this would be very difficult and some of the implant could be left behind. If you are concerned about keloids I would perform the explant using an infra-mammary incision as a keloid in this area is easier to cover up than an armpit keloid. The armpit keloid would be visible in sleeveless tops, bras, swim wear, etc. 
Is it possible to have a tummy tuck after colon resection?
Tummy Tuck After Colon Resection and Hernia Repairs
Jan 15, 2018
If you do not have a colostomy or current abdominal hernias then you may be a candidate for a tummy tuck. Please see a board certified and experienced plastic surgeon as a consultation is required to determine if you can have the tummy tuck. 
If implants are above the muscle, is there still a capsule to be removed?
Capsulectomy
Jan 15, 2018
All breast implants are surrounded by a capsule but not all capsules need removal. If the capsule produces excessive firmness, distorts the shape of the breast implant or causes pain then a capsulectomy is required to improve these conditions. Please see a plastic surgeon in your area if you have any concerns.
