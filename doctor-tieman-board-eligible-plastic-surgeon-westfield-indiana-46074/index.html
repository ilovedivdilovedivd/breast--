Is 6.5 hours for a mommy makeover the amount of time I will be under anesthesia?
This is dependent on the plastic surgeon
Jul 25, 2022
Typically speaking the quoted time for surgery is total room time meaning from the time you roll into the room until you roll out of the room after your surgery. Most plastic surgery literature will state that you want to keep your elective surgeries under 6 hours total of general anesthetic to avoid increased risks of complications, so your surgery would fall in that category. Each surgery has to be evaluated on a patient-to-patient basis. However, some surgeons may push that time limit.
If I get Botox would it help eliminate/reduce the body odour problem too?
Body odor can be caused by a variety of issues
Jul 25, 2022
Body odor can be caused by a variety of issues. However, sweat mixing with bacteria in your armpits and other areas is what leads to “body odor”. Botox can reduce the amount of sweat that is produced and as a happy side effect, will lead to less body odor as well.
Can I get botox armpit injections at 6 weeks of pregnancy for sweating?
This is dependent on the plastic surgeon
Jul 25, 2022
Botox during pregnancy is something that each plastic surgeon will have their own personal policy on. Some feel that it is completely safe while others have a strict “no botox during pregnancy” policy. The plastic surgeons that are comfortable injecting botox during pregnancy typically wait until after the first trimester as that is when all the critical structures are formed in the fetus. You should contact your local plastic surgeon’s office and ask them about their policy.
