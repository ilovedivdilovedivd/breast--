Will whitening the back of my tooth help to prevent the veneer shade to get darker?
Whitening with Veneers
Jun 16, 2019
Short and sweet answer here:
The veneer and crown shades should not change in ten years. Your natural teeth will darken over time without whitening.
“Whitening the veneered tooth from behind” should not be considered, unless the tooth had been darkened from injury/root canal treatment. In that case, an internal bleach can help alter a necrotic tooth.
Do any insurance companies pay for 4 and 1 or is considered cosmetic surgery?
All on 4 – Insurance coverage
Jun 14, 2019
An All-on-4 fixed prothesis is typically not covered under dental benefit plans. A traditional set of dentures are usually covered to some degree. Traditional dentures are considered “usual and customary”. An all-on-4 is a far better solution, but is not “usual and customary”. It is a fairly expensive treatment that requires a great deal of doctor training and the use of superior materials and laboratories.

I have All-on-Fours and the abutments show in the top gum. Is this normal?
Abutment showing on top
Jun 9, 2019
Abutments sometimes show in the esthetic zone when tissues heal and recede. With the the proper implant height selection, the final prosthesis should fit the tissues well and not reveal the underlying abutments especially in the maxilla (upper arch).
That being said, each case is different and has it’s own difficulties and challenges. Without radiographs, CBCT imagining, and/or photographs, a comprehensive evaluation with recommendations is difficult.
