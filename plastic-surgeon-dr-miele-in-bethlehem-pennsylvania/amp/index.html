Consider Breast Reconstruction Revision with your Own Tissue
Oct 13, 2017
I am sorry to hear that you are having problems with your implant-based breast reconstruction. If you are considering revision reconstruction at this point you should seek out a plastic surgeon who does autologous reconstruction which uses your body's own tissue. There are several options depending on your body shape and size. The most common region would be from your abdominal region utilizing the skin and fat with or without muscle.
