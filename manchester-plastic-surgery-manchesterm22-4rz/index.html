Doctors

James Murphy, FRCS(Plast)

Name: James Murphy, FRCS(Plast)
Statement: Dr. James Murphy is a GMC-registered plastic surgeon based in Manchester, England. He is skilled in all face and body cosmetic procedures, but he has special expertise in mummy makeovers, breast enhancement, and rhinoplasty. His private practice, Manchester Plastic Surgery, features the Vectra 3D, a highly advanced simulation imaging system that helps patients visualise an accurate depiction of their surgery results. It is also one of the few UK-based plastic surgery practices to offer this technology.

After earning his medical degree from Bristol University Medical School, Dr. Murphy received fellowship training at the Royal College of Surgeons of England. He then went on to obtain his Master of Surgery degree for research at Stoke Mandeville Hospital in Buckinghamshire. In 2004, the same year he founded Manchester Plastic Surgery, Dr. Murphy was granted the position of consultant plastic and reconstructive surgeon at Christie Hospital in Manchester. Six years later, he left the position to concentrate on his private practice full-time.

Dr. Murphy has been named one of the best body surgeons in the United Kingdom by Tatler magazine. He is a member of the British Association of Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgeons, the United Kingdom Association of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons, and the British Association of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons.
