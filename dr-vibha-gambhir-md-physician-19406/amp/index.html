How can I improve the wrinkles under my right eye without Botox/Xeomin?
Under Eyes
Nov 22, 2023
Hello, and thank you for your inquiry! PRP/PRF (platelet rich plasma/platelet rich fibrin) would be a good natural solution to your under eye concerns. This procedure uses your own blood to build collagen. This will help with the quality of skin under your eyes. Good luck on your journey!
Fine lines and crepiness under eye area. Which treatment is most effective?
Under Eye Concerns
Oct 26, 2023
Hello, and thank you for your inquiry! Under eye issues can be treated in a multitude of ways. Firstly, you can do a series of PRP/PRF treatments. Your blood will be drawn, spun down, and then injected under the eyes. You will be swollen for a few days/up to two weeks. This procedure should ideally be repeated in a series of 3-4. Secondly, you can do a CO2 laser under the eyes. This will help tighten up the skin and help with those fine lines. Both are great options. Good luck on your journey!
What can I do to minimize the appearance of acne scars after Accutane?
Acne Scarring
Oct 26, 2023
Hello, and thank you for your inquiry! Microneedling is a good treatment for acne scarring. This procedure will need to be repeated a few times. Microneedling can be done 4 weeks apart. Another treatment for acne scarring is a product called Skin vive. Skin vive is a a hyaluronic acid injectable that can help improve the appearance of your skin. Good luck on your journey!
I’ve severe hair loss at the age of 21 . Doctor prescribed PRP therapy. What should I do?
Hair Loss
Oct 26, 2023
Hello, and thank you for your inquiry! PRP is a great option for hair loss. Most patients feel comfortable with these types of treatments because they are using their own blood components to stimulate their hair growth rather than taking medications or using chemicals on their scalp. Good luck on your journey!
