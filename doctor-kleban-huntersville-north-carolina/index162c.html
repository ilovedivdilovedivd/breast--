Fixing dent in butt caused by steroid shot?
Steroid Divot
Nov 14, 2023
A small area of fat loss in the buttocks like this could be treated through fat grafting or by utilizing an off-the-shelf product such as Renuva. Fat grafting requires liposuction from somewhere else to collect fat to inject into the area of volume loss, whereas Renuva can be injected much like the fillers we use in the face.
En bloc & capsulotomy – confusion on types of implant removal procedures
En bloc vs total capsulectomy
Dec 19, 2022
This terminology can definitely be confusing. An en bloc capsulectomy means that the surgeon will remove the implant and the capsule in one piece without removing the implant from the capsule. A total capsulectomy means that the surgeon will remove all of the capsule and the implant. The capsule may come out in more than one piece or the implant may be removed to safely get the remaining capsule out. An en bloc is a type of total capsulectomy.
