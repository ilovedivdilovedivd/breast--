How long should I wait after CO2 Laser to have a Deep Plane Facelift?
Wait until skin recovery to undergo a facelift–usually after a month.
Nov 8, 2023
It is best to wait until you have healed completely from the laser treatment (usually after one month) to have any surgery to the face. If performed too early, it would be dangerous to have a surgical face lift immediately after a CO2 laser treatment as this would compromise the healing of your incisions. Once the skin has recovered sufficiently, you will be able to undergo a facelift without issue.
Will a facelift work to address jowls, double chin and volume loss?
To address sagging, jowling and volume think: Facelift/Necklift plus Fat Transfer
Oct 24, 2023
A facelift will address sagging cheeks and jowling by bringing the deep tissues back into position after years of gravity and aging. A double chin may be addressed by a necklift that lifts and tightens the deep tissues and removes unwanted fat. Volume loss in the midface (cheek area) can be addressed with fat transfer. Undereye aging will likely need a lower eye lift.
Will the eye shape be changed with a Midface lift (MFL) performed from incision under the
Eye shape may change with this approach
Oct 10, 2023
The midface lift approach is a good option for those who have simple descent of the midface and in particular the malar fat pad. When skin resection is necessary, this may need to be carried to the undereye. When this is done, it may alter the appearance of the lower lid (however, perhaps not in a bad way). I would look at before and afters from your surgeon to get a sense of what is achievable with this technique.

Cheekbone widened after deep plane facelift 3 years post-op
Cheekbone widened from lifting of deep layers of tissues
Oct 8, 2023
The widening of your cheek bone is likely from the “bunching” of the deep layers of tissues at the point where they are anchored into the bone. The only fix for this would be a surgical one where the tissues are carefully thinned. Given that this area is high risk for nerve damage as the frontal branch of the facial nerve passes along here, I would advise having the procedure performed by a skilled and experienced facelift surgeon. Steroid injections are unlikely to help.
Can a deep plane facelift be performed 2.5 years after a parotidectomy?
Facelift may still be performed after parotidectomy
Oct 8, 2023
It is true that having had a parotidectomy puts you at higher risk of nerve damage as the facial nerve and its branches run through the parotid gland. It would be important to know if you had a superficial or total parotidectomy as this would indicate how much of the parotid gland was removed. Either way, a facelift may still be performed by an experienced and skilled facelift surgeon who is comfortable with the facial nerve anatomy.
