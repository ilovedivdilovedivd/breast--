Statement: Robert Herbstman, MD, FACS
Dr. Herbstman is the Medical Director/Owner of Contemporary Plastic Surgery (CPS) in New Jersey. Dr. Herbstman is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and is a past president of the New Jersey Society of Plastic Surgeons. In addition, he is facility Inspector for the American Association for Accredited Ambulatory Surgery Facilities having served on the Safety and Standards Committee. He has served as an Assistant Professor of Plastic Surgery at Robert Wood Johnson Medical School. Dr. Herbstman is named amongst Top Cosmetic Surgeons by Castle Connolly for past 15 years and has been listed as a Top Plastic Surgeon by NY Magazine and NJ Monthly Magazine . He specializes in artfully performed cosmetic surgery of the face and body having performed hundreds of facial enhancement procedures as well as breast and body contouring including mommy makeovers
