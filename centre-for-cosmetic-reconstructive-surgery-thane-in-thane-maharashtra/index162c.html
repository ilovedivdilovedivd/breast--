Name: Sameer Karkhanis, MS, DNB
Statement: Dr. Sameer Karkhanis, MS, DNB is the chief plastic surgeon and director of the Centre for Cosmetic and Reconstructive Surgery in Thane, India. Though he offers and performs a wide range of surgical procedures across the face, breast, and body, he is best known for breast enhancement, body contouring, and aesthetic facial surgeries, including facelifts and blepharoplasty. He is also recognized by his Mumbai area patients for his warm and attentive bedside manner. “I believe in providing the optimum care to restore health, renew confidence, and enhance beauty — one patient at a time,” says Dr. Karkhanis.

Dr. Karkhanis has over 15 years of experience providing patients with natural results. After his initial training in plastic surgery at Lok Nayak Jai Prakash Narayan Hospital through New Delhi’s Maulana Azad Medical College, he completed two fellowships in Brazil (in São Paulo and Curitiba, respectively). Beyond his medical practice, Dr. Karkhanis stays active in the medical community through professional organizations, including the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.
