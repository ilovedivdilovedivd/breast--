Breast augmentation is sometimes also referred as prosthetics in Denver. The choice of implant size is the most important preoperative activity to achieve the best aesthetic result.

What are implants used for breast augmentation in Denver?

There are many types of breast implants, the choice of which is carried out together with the plastic surgeon, starting from the original data and goals that you want to achieve with the help of breast enlargement.

The shape of breast implants

Breast implants may vary in shape. The most popular are round and teardrop implants (anatomical) shape.

Bigger Boobs is for women who are not happy with their natural breast size

Round breast implants provide a known effect of bras “push-up”, creating a large volume in the upper breast. Some women likes this effect, while others believe that the breast looks an unnatural.

Anatomical (teardrop shaped) implants give a greater volume of the lower part of the breast, simulating a breast sagging under the force of gravity. At the same time when a woman lies, the lower part of the breast all the same remains more voluminous, while natural breasts acquires the uniform rounded shape.

Selecting the shape of the implant will depend not only on your preferences, but also on your individual anatomy. You need to listen to the advice of your plastic surgeon.

The volume of breast implants

Boob Enhancement CO for fuller, natural-looking breast contours

The volume of breast implant is measured in milliliters (cubic centimeters). When you select a volume of implant should be guided by your original dimensions and the goals you want to achieve with the help of boob augmentation. Your plastic surgeon may recommend more or less volumetric implant depending on your anatomy.

The filler of breast implants is silicone gel or saline. Currently, all breast implants are composed of silicone shell with a filler. The most popular fillers for breast implants this silicone gel (or silicon), and saline (or physiological solution).

Silicone gel in its consistency reminiscent of jelly, whereby rather accurately simulates the natural consistency of the breast. Such implants are more pleasant to the touch. At rupture of the silicone implant, a small amount of silicone flows into the surrounding tissue, which can cause morbid sensations and breast shape change.

However, often, the rupture of the implant is flowing asymptomatically and unnoticed both for the woman and for her doctor.

Breast Augmentation Enlargement Denver, CO for women whose breasts have deflated after pregnancy

In order to timely detect possible rupture of the implant, your doctor may recommend periodic examination once in 2-3 years.

A saline solution (physiological saline) is in fact a salt water, a little worse consistency mimicking natural breast.

In case of rupture of the implant with saline solution, liquid flows out into the surrounding tissue and painlessly absorbed into the bloodstream and do not pose any threat to women’s health.

In this situation it may require a second operation for extracting the silicone shell of the implant, and the replacement of the damaged implant a new one.

Where will the scar after the boob enlargement?

There are several ways to install breast implants, and the location of the place of postoperative scar will depend on the chosen method.

Breast Enhancements Scar

Through an incision in the fold under the breast (inframammary access). This is the most common surgical approach in Denver, which is recommended for women planning future breastfeeding. The main drawback of this access is rather noticeable scar under the breast.

Through the incision along the edge of the areola (periareolar approach). Is technically more difficult access, the main advantage of which is that the scar after the operation will be virtually unnoticeable. However, such access is not recommended, if the woman is planning to breastfeed in the future. The fact is that during bust enhancement there is a risk of damaging the milk ducts.

Breastfeeding is practically impossible. Furthermore, there is a risk reduction the nipple sensitivity.

Through an incision to the armpit (transaxillary access). Technically is the most difficult access, for the implementation of which requires a special equipment (an endoscope). The main advantage of this access is the scar after surgery almost imperceptible.
