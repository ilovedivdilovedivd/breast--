Answer: Do solid silicone pec implants need to be replaced every 10-20 years like breast implants?
Sep 20, 2023
Hi Kurin,Thank you for the question. Pectoral implants are different from breast implants in that they are form stable, meaning that they do not rupture and leak. In fact, we sometimes even have to make small adjustments to pec implants intraoperatively by carving them in order to fit an individual, something you could not do with breast implants.
