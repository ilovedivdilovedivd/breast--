5'4, 130lbs, ticklish stomach after lipo 1 year ago, is it going to go away?
Liposuction sensitivity
Jul 5, 2023
Thank you for your question. Many people experience increase sensitivity after liposuction. This is due to the nerves of the skin regenerating. This process can take up to two years to resolve. If you still experience this after two years, it is most likely permanent at that point. You still have more time to heal so stay positive! Best of luck.
