Doctors

John Paul Tutela, MD

Name: John Paul Tutela, MD
Statement: Dr. John Paul Tutela is a board-certified plastic surgeon with offices in New York City and throughout New Jersey. His reputation for creating customized protocols using superior surgical techniques to reveal truly natural results has made him a sought-after plastic surgeons in the Tri-State area.

After receiving his MD from Rutgers University Medical School in Newark, New Jersey, Dr. Tutela completed his residency in General Surgery at Saint Vincent’s Hospital and Medical Center of Manhattan, then went on to become General Surgery chief resident at Yale New Haven Hospital at Yale University.

Following his residency, he completed a plastic surgery fellowship at the University of Louisville Hospital in Kentucky and a postdoctoral research fellowship with a focus on wound healing at New York University-Langone Medical Center, Institute of Reconstructive Plastic Surgery Microvascular and Tissue Engineering Laboratory.

Dr. Tutela is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the recipient of numerous awards, including the 2015 West Orange Doctor’s Choice Award Winner in Plastic Surgery.

He believes that in order for patients to have truly transformative results, they must embrace surgery with a clear mind and healthy body. He emphasizes a holistic approach to nutrition, exercise, and wellness alongside the procedures he offers in his facilities, working with an expert team of practitioners to provide the very best care at every stage of a patient’s journey.
