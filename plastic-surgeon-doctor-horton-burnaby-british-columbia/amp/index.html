Can I use both xiamen and allergan botox at the same time?
Using Xeomin and Botox at same time
Dec 5, 2014
Although both neuromodulators, Botox and Xeomin, are made by different companies they are virtually the same. Botox has some stabilizing proteins that Xeomin does not but other than that they are the same. They are safe to use close together. The doses of these products used for cosmetic purposes are usually very low. For “therapeutic” doses they are often 10X more.
