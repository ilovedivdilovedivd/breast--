Does it matter whether I get SculpSure done 20 lb. to goal or wait until I’m at goal re: long-term vi
SculpSure before getting to goal weight
Mar 8, 2018
You do not have to wait until you get to your goal to see good results with SculpSure. SculpSure permanently destroys fat cells so having the procedure now should help improve your appearance now and you will look even better as you lose more weight.

Price of SculpSure

What is the difference in results for SculpSure Laser Fat Loss vs. Coolsculpting?
SculpSure vs CoolSculpting
Mar 6, 2018
SculpSure offers several advantages over CoolSculpting. Treatment time with SculpSure is about half that of CoolSculpting. There is no down time so you can resume normal activity right after your treatment. You can have it done on your lunch hour and then go back to work. There is no skin or nerve damage with SculpSure. Be sure to do your research before deciding but I believe SculpSure is a better option.
SculpSure (or CoolSculpting) or liposuction?
SculpSure vs CoolSculpting
Mar 6, 2018
Since you have already tried liposuction and CoolSculpting you might want to try SculpSure. SculpSure is a completely different noninvasive technology than CoolSculpting so you may see different results.
Sculpsure applicator placement
SculpSure applicator placement.
Mar 6, 2018
The placement of the applicators is at the discretion of the practitioner based on each individuals body type. Horizontal placement of the applicators will produce the same results as vertical placement assuming the applicators were placed over the proper area.
SculpSure: Are 2 treatments needed 100% of the time in 100% of clients?
Are two SculpSure treatments necessary?
Feb 27, 2018
You may see some results with just one treatment but no one can say for sure. The results are clearly better with two treatments. If you are going to do SculpSure you should really plan on doing two treatments however two treatments are not a requirement.
SculpSure aftercare: Should I not eat anti-inflammatory food as well?
Diet after SculpSure
Feb 19, 2018
Your diet should not affect your SculpSure results. Especially if it is a healthy diet. This will only help to improve your desired effect.
