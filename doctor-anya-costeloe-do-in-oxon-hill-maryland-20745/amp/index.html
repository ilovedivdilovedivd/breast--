Hemosiderin stain – is there any treatment for this problem?
Hemosiderin stain treatment
Jan 26, 2023
Hemosiderin stain treatment There is a difference between bruises (ecchymosis) and hemosiderin stain. Bruising is the blue/purple/brown discoloration that resolves in days to weeks. Hemosiderin stain is a dark discoloration of skin, and can take months or years to resolve. Sometimes it can be permanent. Hemosiderin is a compound that helps with storing iron in the human body.
