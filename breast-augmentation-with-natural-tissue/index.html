Breast Augmentation with Minimal Natural Breast Tissue?

Natural Looking Breast Augmentation recovery

Ample breast tissue is not necessary for a breast to look natural after a breast augmentation. Even, men with zero breast tissue sometimes get implants and can look natural. Suggestions to achieve a natural appearance are:

  1. Choose aconservative size. It is much easier to create a natural looking breast when the patient has significant breast tissue. A larger implant will look like a mound on the chest, however, with minimal tissue.
  2. Use a silicone implant, which is less likely to show wrinkles, and it will feel more natural,
  3. Place the implant under the pectoralis muscle so that she has more tissue covering the implants.
  4. Consult a plastic surgeon certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. (E. Ronald Finger, MD, Savannah Plastic Surgeon)

Natural breast looking augmentation for A-cup patients

Natural Looking Breast Augmentation photo

When there is essentially no breast tissue to begin with and the chest/breast skin and soft-tissue is very tight the size/volume of the implant that will be needed to create a natural looking breast is smaller than usual.

I would recommend choosing silicone implants – either gummy bear type implants or form stable implants. These should be placed under the muscle and should be moderate in size if a natural result is desired, knowing that if additional volume is desired an implant exchange down the road may be needed.

A most natural looking breast implants result is very possible in a thin woman

To achieve a very natural result in your fiance, it is important for the surgeon to pick a range of implants that will fit well for her specific her body type. I consider two factors when selecting implants for my patients:

  1. Dimensional planning
  2. Gel Implant Sizing system

Dimensional planning – The measurements of her chest wall are taken. Also, the breast dimensions including the height, width, and current dimensions of each breast form the basis of dimensional planning.

Based on these measurements, the implant size is recommended. This will give her a unique breast implant that is suited for her body frame. Even her fitness levels and other relevant criteria are taken into consideration.

Gel Implant Sizing system – During the preliminary breast implant consultation, she will be provided with an option to “try on” a variety of implant shapes and sizes. She can also visualize the possible outcomes of her surgery which helps her to get that perfect size to give her the shape that she has longed for. This way her preferences are known and she can then pick a range of implants that will “fit” just right to give a most natural looking breast implants fuller look. (C. Breast enlargement in thin women with thin tissue can look natural

Thin women with minimal breast tissue and tight skin can achieve a natural, pleasing natural looking breast enlargement by following a few simple guidelines.

After careful measurements, choose an implant whose width closely matches the patients breast width.

This will look the most proportional and likely will not be oversized for the patient either.

Choosing a smaller implant won’t put too much tension on the tight skin. The skin will accomadate to the new volume and relax over a few months.

Request the implant be placed under the chest muscle as this gives the most cover of the implant and will best camoflouge potential rippling frequently seen in thin skinned patients.

And finally, as long as she is over 22 years old, strongly consider silicone implants after a full consultation with your plastic surgeon as the feel is more natural and rippling is less than with saline. (Jeffrey M. Darrow, MD, Boston Plastic Surgeon)

Breast Implants for Small Breasted Slight Women Works Well

For small breasted women with minimal body fat and small frames, such as in many Asian females, a high profile silicone gel implant provides excellent results. Measurements of the chest wall with consideration of the patient’s height and weight are key, and in those patients that have small chest walls, a high profile breast implant combines a narrow base (diameter of the implant) with the ability to provide cleavage without looking too “busty”.

The silicone gel implant has less chance of rippling as is occasionally seen in those patients that start out with little to no breast tissue and minimal subcutaneous fat and undergo saline breast enlargement. I believe that such an operation would greatly increase your girlfriend’s self esteem and provide considerable “spring in her step”.(Daniel Shapiro, MD, Scottsdale Plastic Surgeon)
