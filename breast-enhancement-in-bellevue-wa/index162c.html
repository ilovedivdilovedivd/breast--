Cost Of Breast Enhancement In Bellevue

Breast augmentation in Bellevue is a plastic surgery procedure to increase breast size and enhance breast shape, typically through the placement of silicone breast implants

Psychologists proved, any woman associates her femininity and beauty of your own body with a breast.

Therefore, such a reverent attitude to her breast and a desire to make her beautiful and to give a rounded shape anatomically correct is a naturally!

Plastic surgeons and mammoplasty (breast surgery) can help you in this.

The purpose of mammoplasty in Bellevue is a correction of the form of and breast size and giving it maximally natural aesthetic appearance, proportionate physique of the patient.

Indications for breast aug:

  1. the small size of the breast;
  2. a ptosis (sagging breasts);
  3. disproportionately large breasts.

The reasons which have caused this or that deformation a lot. It may be an innate feature, the result of weight loss, a consequence of pregnancy and lactation, aging (hyperextension of the skin and ptosis breasts under the influence of gravity).

The lift of sagging breasts often is combined with the installation of the implants. The implant restores the shape of the breast, a mastopexy corrects the excess skin. As a result, the patient receives visible a vertical scar on the breast, but has an attractive breast.

Bellevue Plastic Surgery Practice

Reduction mammoplasty is performed for medical indications. In the patients can observed curvature of the spine,chest deformation, impaired respiratory function, etc. due to excessively large weight of breast.

For breast reduction a lower third of the breast is removed, and the excess skin is excised. This operation is similar to the technique of execution with a mastopexy (an incision around the areola and a vertical scar is down).

Postoperative recovery.

The patient is in a hospital clinic 3 days and then she can go home. Pain in the chest area completely passes through the week. Sutures are removed 8-10 days. In the first few weeks after surgery the swelling is saved (chest swells) and seems more solid and complete. Form breast will change for the better, in process the healing of scars.

Bellevue, Washington Breast Augmentation Using Silicone Gel

You should definitely to wearing a compression underwear 4-6 weeks after breast surgery (special surgical supporting bra, like a sports T-shirt).

Any physical activity is contraindicated and should be restricted the active exercise, especially the movement of the hands.

The ideal breast: on the results of mammoplasty.

Bellevue breast augmentation results are always visible. Rate it first effect of breast correction is possible in 2-3 months, and the final aesthetic result comes after complete healing of scars (6-12 months).

The operation’s success is determined by the naturalness of received breast contour. The more natural looking woman’s breasts, the higher the quality of the work done by the surgeon.

Breast Implant Surgery Can Achieve Dramatic And Beautiful Result

However, no doctor will provide a lifetime warranty on the beautiful shape of the breast, as well as “a lifetime warranty on a youth.” The breast will age as well as the entire body (sagging under gravity or after pregnancy and lactation). So, many women in Bellevue are doing repeated breast enhancement, it can happen in 7-10 years, maybe in 20 years. Such operations are successfully practiced by plastic surgeons all over the world.
