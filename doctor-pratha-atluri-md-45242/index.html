What type of lipo/Vaser, Smart lipo is the BEST method to get rid of back rolls and get a flat tummy?
Best lipo to get rid of back fat.
Aug 28, 2018
Results of lipo depend mostly on the doctor doing the lipo than the tool used to do it. However, if the choice was between Smartlipo (laser) versus Vaser Lipo, (Ultrasound) hands down, Vaser would be the winner when it comes to back fat. Forehead acne scars: harder to treat?
Forehead Acne Scars
Aug 25, 2018
Ice pick scars are the most resistant ones to treatment. If your forehead scars appear to be wide boxcar type, try stretching then with your two fingers in front of a mirror. If the appearance of the scar improves, those scars can be lifted with minor subsicion and Bellafill filler. Bellafill is FDA approved for acne scars, and wide boxcar, atrophic and rolling scars respond well.
