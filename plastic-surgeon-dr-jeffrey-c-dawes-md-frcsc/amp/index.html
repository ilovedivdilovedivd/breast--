CoolSculping or TempSure?
Skin Tightening

The most effective option for skin tightening is surgical removal of the excess skin. I see you are specifically referring to the abdominal area this would generally be a tummy tuck or in some cases only a mini tummy tuck is needed. I'm trying laser at home. Is it working?
Laser hair removal
Sep 21, 2020
Although I cannot comment on the device you are using at home as it is not something we recommend to our patients. I can comment on laser hair removal. Once treated with laser hair removal, treated hair may take up to 4 weeks, or in some cases, as little as a few days to fall out following treatment. Do not be alarmed if hairs appear to be “growing” during this time.

Arms are very saggy. When doing CoolSculpting should the machine be in the saggy part or th
Coolsculpting Arms
Aug 14, 2020
Coolsculpting is an excellent treatment to help reduce unwanted volume in the arms caused by excess fat tissue. However, if you have loose skin before treatment you may find it more noticeable after as there is less volume and more skin is left hanging. I would recommend you seek a consultation with a plastic surgeon to address surgical options for removing excess skin, to achieve the firmer arm look you are seeking.
​How soon after getting Halo to my face can I do lap swimming underwater with goggles?​
Swimming after Halo
Aug 10, 2020
There is no reason you cannot return to swimming once fully healed from your Halo laser treatment. We advise patients to avoid skin irritants on the treated area for one month after Halo. After one month you should be able to safely swim again. However let your skin be your guide if your skin becomes irritated you may need to wait longer.
