Can EmSculpt Neo cause burns?
Yes it is possible.
Jul 30, 2021
Yes it is possible for the radio frequency to cause skin burns. It is pretty unlikely as the hand pieces have a temperature sensors in them which monitor the tissue and are effective in preventing it from happening. Treating over an area with scar tissue or an area that is numb increases the possibility of a burn.
Fortunately it’s unlikely for these burns to be severe and should heal like any burn in the unlikely event it should occur.
