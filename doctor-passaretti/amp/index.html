Statement: Dr. David Passaretti is a renowned board-certified plastic surgeon with over 15 years in practice. Based in Darien, Connecticut, he serves patients from across the greater Connecticut region.

“Cosmetic surgery can give you added confidence and improve the quality of your life, but no one should ever look as if they had surgery,” Dr. Passaretti says. “The results should be a natural-looking enhancement.”

Dr. Passaretti is especially noted for his excellence in face sculpting procedures such as facelifts and rhinoplasty, as well as breast enhancements (ranging from breast augmentation to breast reduction gynecomastia surgery) and body contouring treatments including liposuction, Brazilian Butt Lifts (BBL), and tummy tucks.

He also offers complete mommy makeovers, a highly customized combination of procedures that creates a dramatic transformation in a single operation.

Dr. Passaretti’s plastic surgery center in Connecticut is equipped with a full medspa that offers a wide range of noninvasive and minimally invasive treatments. These include dermal fillers, neuromodulators like Botox and Dysport, CoolSculpting body contouring, and laser skin rejuvenation.

After earning his medical degree at Tufts University School of Medicine in Boston, Massachusetts, Dr. Passaretti completed a plastic surgery residency at the University of Cincinnati in Ohio.

Today, he is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS). He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

Over the span of his career as a plastic surgeon in Connecticut, Dr. Passaretti has been honored with numerous awards and accolades. He has received the Top Doctor award from Connecticut Magazine every year since 2011. In addition, he has received the Top Doctor award from both New York Magazine and New York Metro every year since 2015. Castle Connolly named him a Regional Top Doctor, and Vitals has given him the Compassionate Doctor Award, Patient Choice Award, and On-Time Doctor Award.

Dr. Passaretti’s own patients have described him as a “standout in his field,” “extremely warm and reassuring,” and a “consummate professional and talented surgeon.” His staff has also been noted for their kindness, compassion, knowledgeability, and friendliness.

Whether you’re seeking a minimally invasive treatment or are ready to discuss a plastic surgery procedure such as mommy makeover, breast augmentation, or a facelift, Dr. Passaretti and his exceptional staff in Darien, Connecticut look forward to discussing your goals during a private consultation.
