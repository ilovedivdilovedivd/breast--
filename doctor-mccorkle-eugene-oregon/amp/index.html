Is it appropriate to only inject Restylane silk into the upper lip?
Is it appropriate to only inject Restylane silk into the upper lip?
Nov 2, 2018
Restylane Silk is an excellent product to use for lip lines when you do not want to add additional volume to your lips. Lips tend to swell when "injured" so your reaction is very normal! Using ice is the easiest and most common way to reduce the swelling. Be patient, apply ice, and communicate with your doctor if the swelling is not resolving within 48 - 72 hours. Good luck!
