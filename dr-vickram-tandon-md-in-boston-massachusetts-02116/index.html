How soon after nanofat transfer can I get filler, Botox & microneedling?
Discuss with your surgeon
Aug 28, 2023
In my practice, after fat transfer, I allow patients to have Botox or microneedling at the same time or any time post-procedurally. With regards to filler, I would be OK with hyaluronic acid filler to the area 2 months post-procedure, after I have a better idea of how much fat remains following the resolution of swelling. Your surgeon will advise best, as their understanding of your anatomy, and your procedure, will be most pertinent.
Thigh dent 6 months after lipo. Any advice?
Contour irregularities are common after extremity liposuction
Aug 23, 2023
It is important for you and your plastic surgeon to discuss the risks related to aggressive liposuction, as contour irregularities in the thighs are common. To correct this, you would likely need an additional procedure, possibly fat transfer or additional round of liposuction to smooth things out. Morpheus 8 aims to tighten the skin, to a depth of 7-8 mm, so you should discuss how improvement in the skin will address your concerns.
Have my implants bottomed out? 6 months post-op
Breast implants settle over time
Aug 23, 2023
Over time, the swelling of the pectoral muscle resolves and the implant settles as the capsule around the implant matures. It is difficult to assess whether your implants have bottomed out, but I would recommend discussing with your surgeon if the breast aesthetic does not meet your goals.
I was considering a skin only tummy tuck but wondering if this can fix the area above my belly butto
Doubtful!
Aug 23, 2023
Patients with loose skin around their belly button and a diastasis recti (widening of abdominal muscles) generally need a fully tummy tuck to see improvement in the quality of the abdominal contours. The skin-only tucks not only would likely not address the skin quality with any sort of durability, they also would not address the abdominal muscles.
