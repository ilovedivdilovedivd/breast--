Doctors

Guray Yesiladali, MD, FEBOPRAS

Name: Guray Yesiladali, MD, FEBOPRAS
Statement: MD. Güray Yeşiladalı, a graduate of Istanbul University Cerrahpaşa Faculty of Medicine, pursued further education in Germany during his 6th year of training with a scholarship from the Turkish-German Medical Association at Giessen University. He completed his specialization training in plastic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgery at Haydarpaşa Numune Training and Research Hospital.
In the United States, he was accepted as an “International Fellow” in the Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery Department at the University of Wisconsin. With success in written and oral exams in Austria and Belgium, he became a member of the European Association of Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgery (EBOPRAS) and was granted the title of FEBOPRAS.
MD. Yeşiladalı is a member of the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ISAPS), the Turkish Society of Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgeons (TPRECD), the Aesthetic Plastic Surgery Association (EPCD), the European Association of Plastic, Reconstructive, and Aesthetic Surgeons (EBOPRAS), and the Istanbul Medical Chamber.
Güray Yeşiladalı founded Vanity Estetik in 2016 and opened his second hospital in 2023. With a team of 250 people, Vanity provides superior service to patients from all around the world. In this busy schedule, Yeşiladalı maintains motivation and productivity through sports and music. His favorite destinations include the Aegean coasts and Greek Islands, with a passion for sailing and horseback riding.
