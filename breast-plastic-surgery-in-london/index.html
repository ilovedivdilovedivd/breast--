Periareolar access – an incision at the border of the areola and the mammary gland (4cm). Since there is a difference color on the border of the areola and skin, the seam is almost not visible. The restriction is the size areola, less than 4 cm. Is a more complicated and traumatic surgical approach.

Transumbilical approach – an incision made inside the navel. Are very complex traumatic and non-physiological access using an endoscope. This approach is not appropriate for emplacing (pre-filled) silicone-gel implants.
