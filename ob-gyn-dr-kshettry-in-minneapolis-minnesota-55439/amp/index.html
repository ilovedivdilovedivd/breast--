Best Solution for Butt Acne/scars That Leave Marks?
hyperpigmentation and scars on butt
Nov 3, 2018
This occurs on buttocks due to active sebaceous gland infection. Inflammation often leaves hyperpigmentation that fades overtime. We had good results in treating the condition when it occurs, with oral antibiotics for short time, followed by Nd.Yag laser use to coagulate each spot. Then after they dry out, to improve scars we do RF Microneedling. You get less breakout and improve look. Hope this helps you. Topical Retin A use is not effective for this area.
Need advice for stubborn hormonal acne. What options are available?
Battling stubborn acne at age 30 years
Nov 3, 2018
You are on good topical care treatment. If you are having side effects from oral antibiotics, try topical antibiotic. I would check your blood test for total and free testosterone, DHEA, FSH and LH to make sure you do not have polycystic ovarian disease. Birth control pill use can help you and I would increase the dose of spironolactone also to 100mg daily. You can also look into laser or RF Microneedling treatments that helps to clear the skin. Good luck

Can I get Botox and Juvederm with MS?
can I get botox and juvederm with MS.
Sep 29, 2018
Botox or any neurotoxins are contraindicated with multiple sclerosis condition as it can weaken muscle action further. Rarely they can travel to other parts of body and weaken muscles. You can do Juvederm fillers. Check with your doctor also who is caring for MS.
