Is smoking after a breast augmentation detrimental to recovery?
Smoking after breast augmentation
Jul 9, 2017
This is a great question. In general, smoking can be harmful to your tissues especially after surgery because it decreases blood flow. Adequate blood flow is necessary to heal the breast around the implant and at the incision. I recommend that you follow up with your plastic surgeon to make sure you are healing appropriately.

Drain fluid
Jun 8, 2017
Good Morning,
Fluid development and absorption is a natural process of inflammation and healing. Your body tends to make a lot a fluid initially after surgery and that lessens as your tissues begin heal together. Occasionally, the fluid is not completely reabsorbed. Be sure to check back with your plastic surgeon to make sure things are going in the right direction. Hope this is helpful.
