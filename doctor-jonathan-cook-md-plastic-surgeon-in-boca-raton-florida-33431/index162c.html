Timing of Laser after Injectables? Depends on your schedule!
Nov 3, 2021
Hi and thanks for your question! There is no need to delay a laser peel after having had Botox or fillers. Even the deepest lasers (such as a deep resurfacing with Erbium or CO2) are too superficial to affect the products you had injected; in other words, they do not peel deep enough to cause any change to the toxin/fillers. No need to wait to schedule your laser. I hope this helps!
