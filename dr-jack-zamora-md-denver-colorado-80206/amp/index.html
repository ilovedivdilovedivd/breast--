Statement: Dr. Jack Zamora is a board-certified oculofacial plastic surgeon practicing in Denver, Colorado, with over 16 years of experience. Known for his exceptional patient care and innovative cosmetic procedures for the eyes, face, and body, Dr. Zamora is regarded as a pioneer in plasma treatments and stem cell technology.

Dr. Zamora has expertise in blepharoplasty (eyelid lifts), face and neck lifts, FAMI facial fat transfer, and VASER liposuction.

He is also a leader in minimally-invasive treatments. His Cherry Creek and Westminster medical spas offer chemical peels, microdermabrasion, microneedling, and HydraFacials, as well as Botox, dermal fillers, and a wide range of laser treatments and other skin rejuvenation procedures. He also offers NeoGraft FUE hair transplant surgery.

Dr. Zamora is the creator of J-Plazty, a minimally-invasive treatment that uses Renuvion plasma energy subdermally, to instantly rejuvenate and tighten the skin anywhere on the face and body. This revolutionary technique can produce incredible results with minimal incisions, less downtime, and less risk than traditional body sculpting surgeries.

To maximize outcomes, Dr. Zamora often combines this procedure with radiofrequency treatments, resulting in impressive skin tightening. He also offers body sculpting services such as Emsculpt and SculpSure.

After earning his medical degree from Tulane University School of Medicine in New Orleans, Dr. Zamora completed his internal medicine internship at Boston Medical Center, as well as his ophthalmology residency and ophthalmology and oculoplastics fellowship. He is board certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology.

Dr. Zamora partnered with Vitro BioPharma to develop InfiniVive MD, an ultrapure cosmetic serum that results in an unprecedented improvement in wrinkles and other aging signs—a first-of-its kind topical treatment. He has also appeared on the television show The Doctors to discuss the benefits of his J-Plazty procedure.
