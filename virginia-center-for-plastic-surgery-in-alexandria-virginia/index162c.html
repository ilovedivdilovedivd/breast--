Vaser liposuction is a technique that uses ultrasound to break up fat cells and then the disrupted fat gets suctioned out during the procedure. In addition, the heat generated from the ultrasonic waves is supposed to help tighten the skin after the procedure.

In contrast, SAFEELipo breaks up the fat mechanically, sucks out the fat, and then essentially does internal fat grafting in the Fat Equalization phase to even out the results.
