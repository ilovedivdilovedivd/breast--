Is it common to inject filler on one side of the face only?
Yes. You can certainly focus only on one side of the face with filler
Jan 29, 2023
There are times when we try to minimize facial asymmetry with filler much more on one side than another. There are even published reports using filler to minimize something called “Parry-Romberg Hemifacial Atrophy” that is a specific condition where fillers on one side are commonly employed. Usually we use volumetric fillers for this approach.
What can I do to minimize the appearance of pronounced nasolabial folds after 20 lb weight loss? (pho
Filler to soften the fold can look natural
Jan 29, 2023
Don’t try to do more than soften it, as when too much is placed — it can look artificially flat. I like Restylane Defyne and RHA 3 for this area.
How to reduce pore size and help with skin texture? Would the Fraxel work?
Pore minimization is challenging and temporary
Jan 29, 2023
But the treatments that seem to work best are things like Halo (hybrid fractional) and photodynamic therapy (more with Ameluz). Newer treatments just Coming to market line 1726nm lasers (like Accure) can decrease the sebaceous gland size below the skin and thus seemingly decrease their outflow. Finally, longterm use of topical retinoids like Tretinoin has data on shrinking pores.
Is this sunken eyes or hyper pigmentation under my eyes ?
Under-eye discoloration is hard to treat
Jan 29, 2023
Under-eye discoloration is hard to treat, mostly because the cause is multi-factorial. Rubbing (friction causing lichenificarion), volume loss happens over time (though it doesn’t appear that is much of the issue in your photos, however your lashes obscure some of the assessment), and sometimes larger caliber vessels or even hemosiderin leaking from those vessels compounded by friction.
What is the best option to flatten or remove raised scar and redness on the lower lip and chin? 38 y/
Combination approach to scars is best
Jan 29, 2023
Treatments can only minimize but not erase scars. Combination of treatments over many sessions can be helpful including: intralesional steroid, laser assisted drug delivery, pulse dye laser and fractional ablative (when can do downtime) and fractional non-ablative (when only have a few days of downtime available). To minimize pain, I really like the Acclaro UltraClear laser ablative fractional with laser assisted drug delivery.
Which procedure would help address downturned lip corners, asymmetry and volume loss in the same area
There are some fillers approved for the secondary smile line area
Jan 29, 2023
Secondary smile line area is also called (accordion lines). They happen first in animation, but eventually imprint. The key is to avoid placing volume, as filler volume will simply make the cheek look heavier. Instead, fillers shallowly in the area to air brush the lines to minimize their Appearance can be helpful. I like Restylane Refyne for this as it moves well with expression, has stereophotogrammetry studies in this demonstrating subcutaneous support and is even “on-label” for this ind...
Horizontal Indent line under brow. How can this be fixed?
Resurfacing plus filler
Jan 29, 2023
Ablative Erbium resurfacing for the surface, and carefully filler with a cannula (to minimize the risk of getting into A vessel) like Restylane Kysse or RHA Redensity can help.
