All patients will have a certain amount of scarring after an operation, some more than others. Lumps and bumps should resolve the first 3-6 months but it looks as though some of your skin and subcutaneous tissue has 'hung up' over the neck and formed contracture. The chin looks like the majroity of fat is gone but the skin is slightly redundant...
How Long is Breast Implants Recovery Time?
Breast Augmentation Recovery
Jan 24, 2014
Recovery should take 7 dyas if the implants are placed under the muscle with no heavy lifting pushing or pulling >10 lbs, no repetitive motion and no upper extremity exercise. If the implant is placed over the muscle, for some reason, recovery will be slightly faster. Gradual return to normal activity is anticipated.
What Type of Bra Can I Wear After Breast Augmentation?
Post augmentation Bra
Sep 20, 2013
I have my patients wear a sports bra that is comfortable and snaps/zips in the front. I have them wear it as much as they can especially for the first 6 weeks and during sports etc. Personally I don't allow an underwire bra for the first month or so as many patient's complain about discomfort.
Is It OK to Do Native American Sweat Lodges or Sauna After Getting Silicone Breast Implants?
Yes, go sweat in the Lodge
Sep 18, 2013
If your surgeon feels that you have healed sufficiently then go enjoy the Lodge or pool etc. Remember, the tensile strength of your incisions are only 50% at 6 weeks and you only get one chance to heal well the first time. Good luck.
