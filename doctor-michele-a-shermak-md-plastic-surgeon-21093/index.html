Facial asymmetry after buccal fat removal. What are my options? Fat grafting? Fillers?
Facial Asymmetry Emphasized after Buccal Fat Pad Removal
Nov 1, 2023
Hello! Thank you for your question.
Facial asymmetry is absolutely normal. To minimize asymmetry the easiest would be to either fat graft or add filler to the less amplified cheek, just to achieve better balance.
