Small blue vein near nose bridge – is it treatable with laser?
Likely a Branch of the Dorsal Nasal Vessels
Nov 10, 2023
Hello! This looks to me like it is a branch of the dorsal nasal artery or vein, and it is a part of the normal anatomy of your face. In my experience, IPL or vascular lasers do not work great for vessels of this size, but would not necessarily be dangerous to try. I would definitely NOT recommend attempting any type of sclerosant or injection to this area, as the vessels around the nose can connect to vessels in your eyes, and this would put you at risk for injury to the tissues of the nose…

Can dermal filler be used to make nipples appear more erect and plump?
Dermal Filler or Fat Grafting
May 12, 2023
It is possible to use dermal filler to fill out and “plump” nipples after a free nipple graft. Using your own fat as filler in the form of fat grafting to the nipple, or even a small piece of tissue from your skin (a rolled dermal graft) are also options to augment the flattened appearance of a nipple after free nipple grafting.
