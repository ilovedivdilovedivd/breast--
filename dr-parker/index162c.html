Waxing on Accutane (Isotretinoin)
Waxing on Accutane
Nov 23, 2015
Waxing will be tolerated poorly and the skin will be more fragile, getting overly irritated and traumatized while on Accutane. This occurs due to the extreme dryness while on Accutane. Normal skin oils return after about a month off Accutane and waxing may resume then. Laser hair removal is technically contraindicated while on Accutane also, so unfortunately, shaving is the safest option during the 20 weeks of Accutane or any isotretinoin treatment.
