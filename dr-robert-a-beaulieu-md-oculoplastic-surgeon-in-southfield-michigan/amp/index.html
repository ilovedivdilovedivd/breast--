Filler to upper eyelid
May 2, 2022
The upper eyelid crease is formed by eyelid structures (septum and levator aponeurosis) and their attachments to the skin. This is an anatomically derived line. Filler to the upper eyelids must be done very carefully as it can easily lead to unwanted effects due to the eyelid having very thin skin.
