Numbing after a mini facelift: I am concerned I'm going to be permanently numb.
Normal Healing after a Facelift
Jun 1, 2016
Hi,Normal healing after a facelift consists of swelling, bruising, and diminished sensation of the skin in front of the ear. Bruises resolve over the course of 2 to 3 weeks. Swelling resolves over the course of a few months though most of it goes away during the first few weeks. Diminished sensation resolves more slowly over the course of months. There are rare cases where the loss of sensation can persist but this is unusual.Best of luck with your healing.

Is it ok to have a mini face lift (short scar facelift) at 33 years old?
Best Time for a Facelift
Jun 1, 2016
The time to consider facelift surgery is when there is laxity of the soft tissues of the cheek and loss of definition of the jawline. Neck laxity is also commonly present. It is less a matter of chronological age. I've had patients in their 30's who needed it and others in their 50's who were not ready yet. You shouldn't be dissuaded based on your age.
