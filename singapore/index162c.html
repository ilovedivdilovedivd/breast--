Breast implants can be placed through the different types of access:

Inframammary access is located under the breast, near the bottom of the inframammary fold. This incision is the most common and provides the best opportunity for accurate implant placement and access. This technique is most preferable when working with silicone gel implants, as an incision make 4 cm length. Stitches on the breast are small in size after this type of access.

Periareolar access is made on the edge of the areola. This incision is the best, when the correction the position of the breast or mastopexy (breast lift) will assigned. The incision is usually located on a circle at the bottom or middle part of the circumference of the of the areola. A border-line incision along the periphery of the areola, with this method, it is often less visible than the inframammary access in women with light pigmentation the areola. The probability of a slight reduction in nipple sensitivity higher with this method.

Transaxillary access – an incision made to the armpit and the dissection of the skin made in the middle. This method contributes to the placement of the implant in the absence of visible scars on the breast. However, the percentage complications such as the location of the implants at different heights much higher. Periareolar or inframammary incision (but not always) may required for implant position correction. Transaxillary incision can be performed with or without an endoscopic access, so-called an endoscopic breast augmentation (price for an operation in the Garden City is available on the menu item “prices”).

Transumbilical: a trans-umbilical breast augmentation (TUBA) is a less common implant-device emplacement technique an incision is located in the navel and dissection extends upward. This method allows to place the implant in the absence of visible scar on the breast, but this breast enlargement is very difficult to perform. This access is carried out directly or by means of an endoscope. This method is unacceptable when using silicone gel implants because of the likelihood of the implant shell damage. Therefore, the silicone shell is used, which are filled with saline.

Transabdominal (TABA) is an operation similar to TUBA, when the implants are installed through the abdomen through direct incisions, whilst the patient simultaneously undergoes an abdominoplasty.

A breast implant is a prosthesis used to increase breast size for aesthetic purposes, for the reconstruction of the breast (eg after a mastectomy; or to correct genetic defects), or during surgery to change sex.

There are two basic types of breast implants: saline and silicone-gel. Saline implant consists of elastic silicone shell filled with sterile saline. Silicone gel implants are composed of silicone shell filled with a viscous silicone gel. Besides these, there are other, less common types of breast implants filled with soy oil or polypropylene.
