THERMIva treats vaginal dryness
Feb 19, 2017
THERMIva is a painless, nonsurgical series of 3 treatments that rejuvenate the vaginal tissues helping to restore the normal tone and secretions. It works by heating the collagen which causes remodeling. As this process is happening, new collagen, blood vessels, and nerves regenerate. The result is an increase in secretions, tissue flexibility, and tone and a decrease in laxity. The duration of results are individual but likely lasting 12-24 months.
