Galaflex internal mesh with breast implants, could it be painful & detectable to touch?
Galamesh breast augmentation
Feb 7, 2023
Often times, an absorbable mesh product is utilized in augmentation cases to assist in long term positioning of the implant in relation to the nipple and re-draped skin. Yes, any implant could potentially be detected with palpation by the savvy provider, however I doubt that you will have much trouble with this product as we have used it hundreds of times in small and large busted women without issue and with good cosmetic results.
