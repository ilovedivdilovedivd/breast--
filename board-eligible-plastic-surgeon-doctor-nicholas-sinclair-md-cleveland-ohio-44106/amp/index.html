Question about fat removal options before pregnancy?
Wait till after pregnancy!
Oct 16, 2023
Thank you for your question. It is difficult to offer advice without photos; however, the best advice is to wait until after you are done with pregnancy before attempting any body contouring. Your body will undergo huge changes during the pregnancy and in the post-natal period! It is possible that you find yourself with new aesthetic issues / goals after the pregnancy. You will be best off getting through the pregnancy safely and then addressing any aesthetic issues.
