Hair loss evidence topical Dutasteride, topical Finasteride mixed with Minoxidil, oral Minoxidil, Saw P
Hair Loss Medicine
May 5, 2020
Minoxidil and Finasteride are the two most commonly prescribed medicines for hair loss, they are generally the most effective while limited side effects. Dutasteride may be stronger but it's chance of side effects are significantly higher, while Saw Palmetto and pumpkin seeds seem to be negligible in comparison. You may also consider PRP injections but otherwise you're doing fine.
Should I wait or can I get a hair transplant now? Am I a good candidate for this operation?
Hair Transplant Candidacy
May 5, 2020
Generally we recommend patients not get a hair transplant until their hair loss has stabilized. If you were to get a transplant and lose hair, you would potentially be left with a halo of hair that would look even worse than before.
