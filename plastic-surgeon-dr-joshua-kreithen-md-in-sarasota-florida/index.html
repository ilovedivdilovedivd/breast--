How much does your breast shrink with just a lift?
Breast Lift
May 13, 2020
Dear Polite,
Your breasts should not decrease in size with a lift. However, due to the lift… the volume (and remaining skin) will be higher up and may give the appearance of shrinking since the “drooping” is resolved. Certainly, you would do well with just a lift… and down the road if you decided to go bigger with implants, you wouldn’t have “burned any bridges.” Hope this helps and good luck!
