Moderate plus or high profile, what would you suggest?
Profile Choice
Dec 29, 2020
Great question that comes up all the time in my practice. In general for a 500 cc implant a high profile would usually work best. But that is based upon an average chest width (and breast base width). Moderate plus can be a great choice if the width of the implant is less that the width of a patient’s breast, even up to 500 ccs.

500ccs should give a very full look, even at 5’8″ and at 195 lbs. Best of luck! Dr. Jim Romanelli
