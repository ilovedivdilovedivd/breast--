Will fat graft help lift the breast?
Fat grafting
Jun 8, 2023
Thank you for your question. Fat grafting in it of itself will not lift the breasts, however, it will help to fill them out and increase the upper pole fullness so that your breasts have a more youthful, perky appearance. While it is difficult to tell with your pictures, it does not appear that you need a breast lift. Good luck.

Almost 4 months post op breast lift/reduction revision with 175cc implants. Is high profile possible? (P
Breast revision
May 30, 2023
Thank you for your question. You are correct that your breasts are bottoming out and making your nipples too high. The correction for this is to decrease the amount of skin along the nipple to IMF. Additionally, as you have a history of bottoming out, placing an internal bra will provide the necessary support that you need. Large implants that are pulling the skin down can increase the veins showing through your skin. You may consider downsizing your implants. Good luck.
Surgery June 12th. Will 405cc be too big and top heavy?
Breast augmentation
May 30, 2023
Thank you for your question. While it is very hard to tell without seeing your true before pictures, the range that is picked out will likely get you close to the after picture that you hope for. Bigger implants, above 350 cc, can lead to an increase chance of asymmetry or bottoming out overtime. A 10 to 30 cc difference on a 400 cc implant is practically in perceptible. Good luck.
What will happen if I don't fix my implants that have bottomed out?
Bottoming out
May 26, 2023
Thank you for your question. Your implants have bottomed out. Wearing a supportive bra can help slow down the process, however, this will continue to get worse over time. Revision surgery could include internal sutures, and also an internal bra to provide support and longer lasting results. Good luck.
Am I good candidate for a breast augmentation?
Breast augmentation.
May 26, 2023
Thank you for your question. You are a good candidate for a breast augmentation, however, to get a fantastic result, performing a small breast lift, either via a peri areolar or more likely a vertical incision would improve your result and make it longer lasting.
