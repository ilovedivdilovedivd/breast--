What is breast augmentation surgery?

Breast augmentation surgery (other names – mammoplasty, breast surgery ) is to install implants containing silicone or saline under the breast tissue or under the the breast muscle. This operation is carried out in order to change the size, shape and volume of the breast.

What kinds of breast surgeries are there?

Breast lift is an operation for lifting and changing the shape of sagging breasts. Simultaneously with breast lift surgery is sometimes performed implant placement. In this case much depends on the anatomy and the patient’s wishes.

How long do breast implants last?

Plastic Surgery For Breast Implants In Kentucky Is Usually Done On An Outpatient Basis

The term of service of the implants can not be predicted. They can be (although not in all cases) to stay for a lifetime. All depends on the manufacturer of implants and other factors.

Duration of breast enlargement: This operation usually takes about one to two hours.

Length of hospital stay: As a rule, you need to be in the hospital overnight.

Anesthesia: General anesthesia

Recovery: Within 4 weeks after the bust enlargement is necessary to wear a supportive bra. In addition, for 4 weeks you can not play sports and lift weights. As a rule, the patient can return to work in 3-7 days.

Risks: When performing breast augmentation in Louisville KY, there are the following risk factors: infection, bleeding, swelling, bruising, hardening of the breast, asymmetry, problems with mammography, autoimmune diseases, cancer.
