Nancy de Kleer, MD, FRCSC (Plast)

Name: Nancy de Kleer, MD, FRCSC (Plast)
Statement: Dr. Nancy de Kleer is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Oakville Plastic Surgery in Oakville, Ontario. As an expert in cosmetic surgery, Dr. de Kleer has extensive training and experience that allows her to take an individualized approach, creating surgical treatment plans unique to each patient’s concerns and desired outcomes.

“I treat every patient with the utmost respect, as I feel it is a privilege to be your doctor,” says Dr. de Kleer. “Patient safety, comfort, and satisfaction are my priorities. I genuinely enjoy meeting and taking care of my patients.”

A Toronto native, Dr. de Kleer received her medical degree from the University of Ontario, graduating with honors. She then completed the university’s prestigious plastic surgery residency, where she learned advanced techniques in plastic and reconstructive surgery during the five-year program.

After her residency, Dr. de Kleer became a trauma surgeon at St. Michael’s Hospital in Toronto. She went into private practice when she opened Oakville Plastic Surgery in 2005.

Dr. de Kleer is board certified in plastic surgery by the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada. She is a member of the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada, the Canadian Society of Plastic Surgeons, the Ontario Society of Plastic Surgeons, the Canadian Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and the Ontario Medical Association. She is also an assistant clinical professor at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario.
