What is a “Botox Lift” or “Botox Brow Lift”?
Botox Brow Lift
Feb 21, 2013
Although it initially sounds paradoxical, Botox can be used to weaken certain muscles to allow a subtle, but pleasing, elevation of the eyebrows.

The frown muscles (corrugators) and some related muscles are the target.

By treating these muscles, a gentle, natural-looking lift can be achieved.

For most patients Botox works 3-4 months.

Feb 20, 2013
Malar festoons are often asymmetric. Their treatment is difficult. In fact, this is an area of facial surgery in need of much better solutions.

Unfortunately, the fluid can not be simply aspirated from festoons.

Can Restylane on the Cheeks Be Removed?
Restylane can be dissolved
Feb 20, 2013
Hyaluronic acid products like Restylane and Juvederm can be dissolved with an enzyme called hyaluronidase.

That said, one would not expect any of the filler injected 7 years ago to still be present.

Laser Resurfacing or Photo-facial for Fair Skin?
Laser and IPL each best for different goals
Feb 20, 2013
Laser resurfacing and IPL are generally oriented toward different goals.

Erbium and CO2 resurfacing lasers can have their best effect on improving skin texture.

Different wavelengths of IPL can be emphasized to address skin pigmentary changes such as dark spots (lentigos), redness from rosacea, and very fine vessels (telangiectasia).

Patients with fair skin tend to have fewer problems with laser skin resurfacing than do those with darker skin types.

What Are the Best Preventative Treatments?
Best Preventative Treatments for Maintaining Healthy Skin
Feb 20, 2013
There are several simple and inexpensive things that can be done to help keep skin healthy and beautiful:

1. Minimize sun exposure

2. Hat, sunglasses, protective activewear, and sunscreen when out in the sun

3. Use a moisturizer with SPF 25 or 30 — every day

4. Avoid smoking

5. Choose a healthy diet with plenty of anti-oxidants

Is It True That Post Care in a Form of a Mask is Compulsory After a Photofacial IPL?
A mask is not compulsory after Photofacial IPL
Feb 20, 2013
No, a mask is definitely not considered a requirement after IPL

The most important thing after IPL is avoidance of significant sun exposure, as the skin will be more photosensitive and could end up with post treatment hyperpigmentation.

Eyelid Fat Repositioning Vs Fat Removal for Eyebags
Lower eyelid fat repositioning and limited fat removal can both be valuable
Feb 20, 2013
There has been a very strong trend in eyelid surgery (and in facial surgery in general) toward fat preservation and away from fat removal.

That said, every case must be individualized. Sometimes fat removal is still a valuable option.

In many cases, I will sparingly remove fat from certain areas and reposition most of the fat over the bony rim.
