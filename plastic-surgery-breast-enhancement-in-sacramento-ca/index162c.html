Plastic Surgery Breast Enhancement In Sacramento, CA

Breast augmentation with implants in Sacramento not only provides a guaranteed effect, but also guarantee 100% safety of the procedure as a whole.

Breast Implants are securely fixed in the tissues without causing rejection by the body, allergic reactions, make it possible to get a natural and aesthetic result.

Clinical trials have confirmed the reliability of modern implants. They can withstand almost any traumatisation level – for example, when a strong impact or drop in breast implants does not will burst, not deformed and will not flow.

In addition, the implant filler – gel is so dense that the probability of spread in the organism – is excluded.

There are two types of modern implants: round and anatomical. Anatomical helps enable maximum natural result, because it simulates the actual shape of the breast.

Breast Augmentation to Enhance body contour and enlarge breasts to a more optimal shape and size

Round implants provide a beautiful round shape and high “young” breast. The volume of implants – from 80 ml to 800 ml, so you can choose the optimal implant for every woman and you can carry out an increase in breast size by 2 or more.

Before the breast enlargement, the patient necessarily has to pass specimen collection and to pass diagnostics. In addition to preliminary surveys you need to take time off from taking certain drugs that affect blood clotting. It is also advisable to give up smoking and alcohol for at least 2 weeks prior to breast enhancement.

It is very important performing weight the correction, if you are planning such, before the boob augmentation, because after it the sharp weight changes may adversely affect the final result.

Breast Implants to Correct partial or total loss of the breast after disease, trauma, or mastectomy

The preoperative consultation with a plastic surgeon you need to give details of taking the drugs, lifestyle, bad habits, chronic diseases and other nuances that affect the result mammoplasty.

Another important point in breast augmentation in Sacramento, CA is the choice of the implant, and place of its installation, and the surgical access method. These decisions are made together with the patient, depending on several factors: what results it expects, natural patient physical data, etc.

For ideal result for each patient is taken 4-8 implant sizes in small steps and the final choice of the implant is held in the operating after being a pocket is formed.

Recovery after breast augmentation

During recovery period is necessary to wear a special compression garment that helps accelerate and improve the recovery period. You may experience pain in the breast and they may seem too large but after elimination of swelling the volume of breast will decrease.

Breast Enhancements to Restore breast size and shape after loss of breast volume post-pregnancy

Pain disappear within 8 to 10 days after boob enlargement and, if necessary, can be easily removed by painkillers.

The sealing and redness of postoperative scars in the first two months after mammoplasty is a normal reaction of the body, they become white and almost invisible within 5-8 months. The scar after breast surgery is virtually invisible month later.

The choice of surgical approach and the installation of the implant

The successful bust enlargement largely depends on the correct choice of the place in which the implant will be installed, and the surgical approach. The selected method of breast augmentation in many ways determine the result, where will be located scarring, whether the woman will retain the ability to breast-feed, etc.

The implant can be installed:

Breast Implant Operation In Sacramento To Balance breast size and correct asymmetry

  1. Under the muscle – implants are protected pectoralis major muscle and breast tissue itself, which excludes its feeling out and displacement.
  2. Under the fascia of the pectoralis major muscle – the method provides an additional fixation of the implant.
  3. Under the glandular tissue – used at presence of a sufficient amount of soft tissue and when a small size breast.
  4. Combined method (dual plane) when the upper part of the implant is placed under the pectoralis major muscle, and the bottom – under the glandular tissue. Some experts believe that such method allows you to securely fix the implant and prevent it from palpation.

The patient’s physical data affect on the choice of the method: the position of the mammary glands, the presence of sagging breasts (ptosis) elasticity and firmness of the skin and others.
