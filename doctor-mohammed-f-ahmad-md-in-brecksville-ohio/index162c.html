Will I get the Tyndall effect with all fillers if I did get it with one?
Tyndalling Effect
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. The tyndalling effect was caused from the filler being placed too superficially. Getting any filler, as long as it's placed correctly, will not cause this.
Will my hair grow back? Is there anything I should do?
Hair
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. I am unable to say whether or not your hair will grow back, not knowing what caused it to fall out. But PRP injections in your scalp can help the hair grow back. This usually takes at least 3 treatment, 6-12 weeks apart.
Can I get lip injections for more symmetry?
Lip Symmetry
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. Lip filler can help make your upper lip a little larger, which will help make it more symmetrical. It is hard to get complete symmetry because with filler alone, it is not possible to change the entire shape of your lip. It can only be used to help make it more even.
What are the benefits for having an Aerolase Neo laser and chemical peel for hyperpigmentation?
Hyperpigmentation
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. The chemical peel will help with the superficial hyperpigmentation and the Aerolase Neo will help with the deeper pigmentation.
How to get rid of redness, pigmentation, and dry dull skin?
Skin Texture
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. I think laser would be a great option for you. Your redness appears to be a touch of rosacea. Doing a laser for skin rejuvenation, such a the Aerolase Neo will help with the overall appearance, texture, and color of your skin.
Dark circles under eyes: What is the best solution when fillers and topicals have failed to work?
Under Eyes
Jul 29, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. You are dealing with a few different issues. First, the pigment is causing the darkness. You need an under eye cream such as ZO Hydrafirm. Second, the hollowness. Injecting combine PRP and filler under your eyes can help with that. By doing both, after about 3 months, you should have great improvement.
How many syringes of filler is needed in the temple area?
Temples
Jul 26, 2019
Hello. Thank you for your question. For the temples, filler isn't always the best option because you need so much. Sculptra is a great option for this area because it will grow your own collagen. Sculptra takes 3 months to show full results.
