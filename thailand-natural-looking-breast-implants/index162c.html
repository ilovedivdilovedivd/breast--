Capsular contracture

Any the foreign material, including the breast implant is perceived by the body as a foreign body. After installing the breast implant develops a local inflammation around it and the shell or a capsule is formed of connective tissue.

Sometimes, however, the capsule can thickens and become very rough, compresses the breast implant and deforms it. This complication is called capsular contracture.

Rupture or leakage of breast implant

Damage to the implant shell may cause the leakage the contents into the surrounding tissue. This complication may occur in the early postoperative period (if the implant has been damaged during augmentation mammoplasty), or after few months or even years after bust enhancement (as a result of wear of the implant shell).
