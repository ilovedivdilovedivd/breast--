For me and my team, patient’s safety is a top priority. I strive to fully understand my patient’s goals, clearly explain and ultimately deliver beautiful results.

Although fully trained in all areas of plastic surgery, my main areas of interest are cosmetic surgery of the breast and body and semi-invasive skin treatments, under Local Anaesthetic. I am one of the few certified Surgeons offering the Allergan Earfold technique, Hair Restoration - FUE with Implanters only-technique and Micro-cannula liposuction under Local anaesthesia for body contouring.
