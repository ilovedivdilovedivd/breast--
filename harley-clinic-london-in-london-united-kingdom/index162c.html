His expertise lies in breast augmentation through breast implants or fat grafting, as well as procedures like tummy tucks, liposuction, complete mummy makeovers, facelifts, and rhinoplasty.

According to Dr. Agha, “Plastic surgery combines art and science with technical precision and a profound understanding of human nature. That’s why I’m so passionate about what I do.”
