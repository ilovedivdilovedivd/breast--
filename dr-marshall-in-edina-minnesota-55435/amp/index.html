What laser is best to address small rosacea areas on the end of the nose?
Best treatment for rosacea and visible vessels
Apr 30, 2018
Hello!
I like to approach rosacea patients with visible vessels by treating the trouble areas with 2 modalities…BBL and vascular laser. The BBL is excellent at improving generalized redness caused by tiny vessels in the epidermis. The vascular laser is a very helpful addition for treating larger vessels. Patients may require 2 to 3 combination treatments spaced 2-3 weeks apart for optimal results.

Best of luck!
Dr. Ann
