Is it possible a chin implant can make the chin smaller?
Is it possible a chin implant can make the chin smaller?
Jan 29, 2023
Hi! A chin implant in this case is counterproductive since the operation involves adding solid silicone to your chin, thus giving it additional volume. The procedure more appropriate for you is osseous genioplasty, which can enhance your facial profile by modifying the skeletal structure of your chin. This allows the surgeon to remove excess bone and make a chin smaller or less prominent.
Is this gynecomastia or not?
Mild case of gynecomastia
Jan 29, 2023
Judging by your photos, I think you have a mild case of gynecomastia. Gynecomastia is the enlargement of the male breasts due to a hormonal imbalance in testosterone and androgen. I think that a simple liposuction procedure is enough to solve your issue since it can already remove the little amount of excess fat in your breasts. A more definitive answer would require an exam by your treating doctor to give you the best answer.
What procedure is this? More than just a tummy tuck?
What procedure is this? More than just a tummy tuck?
Jan 29, 2023
There are multiple tummy tuck techniques. And based on the photo, I’d say that the patient had a standard tummy tuck. This technique tightens the entire abdominal wall – from just above the pubic bone all the way up to a little below the breast bone.

I’d also say that the patient combined her tummy tuck with a liposuction. The two often go hand in hand since liposuction can improve the body contour by eliminating excess fat around the abdomen.

Is it safe to do a lip lift 3 days after rhinoplasty?
Lip Lift After Rhinoplasty
Jan 29, 2023
Hi. The said procedures involve creating multiple incisions that are too close to each other.
