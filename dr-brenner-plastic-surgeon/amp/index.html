Is this nasal valve collapse or can UCL strut be placed unilaterally?
post-operative nasal airway obstruction
Sep 8, 2019
Post-operative nasal airway obstruction can be organic (i.e. anatomic) but can also be due to swelling/edema.  I often will recommend that patients try using Flonase or some other nasal steroid spray after surgery when this occurs. Often times, it will result in some improvement. If the problem is still there one year after surgery, then I may consider going back to taking a look. Of course, an in person evaluation is always important in making these types of decisions.
How many times can a nose be broken before no longer being a rhinoplasty candidate?
nose fractures
Sep 2, 2019
Often times, when patients have multiple injuries to the nose, the structure can be negatively impacted. With a multiply fractured nose, it is not uncommon to require reconstruction with cartilage taken from the ear or rib cage.
Will my breasts look bigger in a few months?
Breasts size change after breast augmentation?
Nov 13, 2017
You are still early post-op after your surgery. From the photos posted, you appear to be healing normally. In general, the breast size will decrease slightly over the first few months as swelling decreases. Although implants will drop over the first few months...this depends on the type of implant (smooth-walled vs. textured surface) as well as pocket placement, subglandular vs. sub-muscular.
13 weeks post op Rhinoplasty and Septoplasty. Will the tip drop any more at this point?
Tip changes after rhinoplasty
Nov 13, 2017
You should continue to have some changes to your tip complex over the course of the first year after rhinoplasty.  In general, the tip complex has usually "settled" by three months. However, further change can occur and will depend on whether or not a cartilage strut was placed, and if so...what the size and shape of it was.
Can harsh nose blowing 2 days after rhinoplasty affect the results?
Blowing your nose after rhinoplasty
Nov 13, 2017
In short, Yes....blowing your nose can be a problem. The most important reason that you should not blow your nose for the first week following rhinoplasty....is that the mucosal incisions are new...and not yet healed. By blowing your nose...you could force them open, possibly exposing your grafts to mucous, bacteria, etc.
What are the chances of hematoma 6 weeks post-op breast augmentation?
Hematoma following breast augmentation
Nov 13, 2017
It would be very unusual....although not entirely impossible....to develop a hematoma 6 weeks following breast augmentation surgery. I usually recommend that my patients wait a minimum of six weeks before initiating any aggressive upper body exercise after breast augmentation.
What is this bump on the tip of my nose after rhinoplasty? 5 weeks post op, visible since week
Nasal Bossae
Nov 13, 2017
Although it is possible to develop visible nasal bossae following rhinoplasty, 6 weeks post-op is a bit early for that. More likely, it may be the suture knot. Absorbable tip sutures usually take 3-4 months to dissolve...of course this depends on the type of suture material.
What happens if I don’t wear a bra after my operation?
Bras after breast augmentation
Nov 1, 2017
Most women prefer to wear a bra after breast augmentation because the support feels good. I usually recommend that my patients wear one...usually a surgical bra or sports bra...so long as there is no underwire...which can dig into the new incision.
Can I soften newly formed scar tissue? I got my breast augmentation 3 weeks ago.
Capsular Contracture after Breast Augmentation
Nov 1, 2017
Capsular Contracture after Breast Augmentation likely has little to do with whether or not you are massaging your implants. Although massaging can and will help to soften normal post-operative scar tissue, if you have a true recurrent capsular contracture...massaging probably will not help.
How long until my breasts drop, and how soon can I start shopping for bras/swimwear get the mos
Breast Implant Settling
Nov 1, 2017
Breast implants often take some time to settle following primary breast augmentation. This is particularly true for smooth walled implant placed in the sub-muscular position. They usually take about 4-5 weeks to drop, but could take longer. Textured implants tend to not ride up high, and stay exactly where they are placed during primary augmentation.
Under eye wrinkles getting worse. Will Botox or fillers help?
Lower eyelid wrinkling
Jun 28, 2017
You need to be particularly careful having Botox injected into the lower lid. Often a better treatment is filling the eyelid-cheek junction...with Restylane (dont use thicker HA fillers for this) or with natural fat transfer....which works great.
Brachioplasty on severe asthmatic?
The answer is a strong....Maybe!
Jun 28, 2017
The truth is, that you would need to have your asthma under control in order to safely have surgery performed in an outpatient setting. If your pulmonary physician believe they can do that, then you should be fine to proceed.
Will I need a breast lift?
No way to avoid a breast lift in this case
Jun 28, 2017
It appears from your photo that you have severe breast sagging (at least grade three breast ptosis). You are unlikely to be pleased with your result with anything short of a full breast lift. If the scars are a deal breaker for you, then you probably should not have surgery...but don't settle for a lesser operation.
Will bilateral nipple sensation return after breast implants?
return of nipple sensation
Jun 28, 2017
It sounds like your numbness may be temporary. If you had intact nipple sensation prior to your most recent removal and replacement of implants, it is likely that you will regain sensation. Sometime the nerves simple get "banged up" or swollen, which can cause temporary numbnes.
