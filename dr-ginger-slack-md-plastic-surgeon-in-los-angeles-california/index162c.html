Dr. Ginger Slack grew up in Northern Virginia and moved to Los Angeles after being selected for the highly competitive early-acceptance to medical school program at the University of Southern California. Dr. Slack has presented papers at the American Society of Reconstructive Microsurgery, American Society for Plastic Surgery, and Plastic Surgery Research Council among others. She has also published papers in leading peer-reviewed journals and textbooks on breast reconstruction and craniofacial reconstruction. She is currently faculty at the UCLA Division of Plastic Surgery.
