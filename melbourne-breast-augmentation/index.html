Melbourne Breast Implants

Breast augmentation in Melbourne is a plastic surgery to correct the shape and volume (size) of the breast.

This operation includes three types of surgical interventions: reduction mammaplasty, breast lift and breast augmentation.

Augmentation mammoplasty is one of the most popular and most sought-after plastic surgery today.

Mammoplasty allows you to correct the natural data and obtain the desired breast size.

What is needed for breast augmentation surgery?

Preparation for plastic surgery breast augmentation in Melbourne begins with a consultation with a surgeon.

Breasts Surgery Often Referred To As A Boob Job

Breast augmentation to the desired sizes (volumes) is a surgical procedure and performed in most cases for aesthetic indications. Lack of volume is the main reason for which is performed breast augmentation. Breast surgery can be carried out for medical reasons: as a result of breast cancer, after a significant injury or breast asymmetry, with significant loss of volume after pregnancy and lactation, and after rapid weight loss. Plastic surgery breast augmentation can also be combined with a breast lift.

Implants for mammoplasty

The only effective and safe way to correct breast size today is the installation of the implants. Implants are “bags” with a textured surface and filled cohesive (dense) gel.

The surface of the implants is designed so that after their installation begins “engraftment” of the implant to the breast tissue which lowers the risk of rejection of the implant by the body.

Breast Implants have a high durability and reliability, they are extremely difficult to damage, but even if that happens, the connective tissue that covers the implant over time will not allow the gel to penetrate into the body.

Moreover, the implant filler is very dense in its structure, which can not spread about. Implants are presented in two forms: round and anatomical. Anatomical implants simulated the natural shape of the female breast and more commonly used. Selection the shape of the implant depends on the body structure and the presence of breast tissue. Consultation with a plastic surgeon will help to finally defined with a choice implant.

During the consultation, the plastic surgeon will choose the most preferred method of access to areas of intervention, the place of installation of the implant, as well as the type of the implant.

Breast augmentation requires a compulsory examination to exclude the risk of negative consequences. The complex surveys and tests includes: blood tests, a coagulogram, mammograms, etc.

The patient finds out all the details about the necessary tests and examinations on the consultation.
