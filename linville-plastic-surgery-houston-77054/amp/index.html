Doctors

Cain R. Linville, MD, FACS

Name: Cain R. Linville, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Cain R. Linville is a double-board certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Linville Plastic Surgery in Houston, Texas. With an inherently artistic eye, a robust educational background, and knowledge of today’s most cutting-edge technologies and techniques, he combines both art and science in every procedure he performs.

Dr. Linville specializes in plastic surgery of the face, breast, and body. He is most noted for achieving exemplary results in breast enhancements, tummy tucks, and mommy makeovers.

“My primary focus is on achieving beautiful, natural-looking results, uniquely tailored to suit your needs,” says Dr. Linville. “My promise is to always treat you as family, to listen, and to provide you with a unique, individualized experience that is unmatched and yields exceptional results.”

Dr. Linville obtained his medical degree at the University of Missouri’s School of Medicine. He then completed a residency in General Surgery at the University of Texas at Houston, followed by a fellowship in Plastic Surgery at Ohio State University and a 10-day fellowship with Operation Smile Stryker in Nicaragua.

Dr. Linville is double board-certified by the American Board of Surgery (General) and the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

Throughout his tenure as a plastic surgeon in Houston, Dr. Linville has been celebrated as a standout doctor. The James “Red” Duke Trauma Service named him Chief Resident of The Year in 2012, and in 2014 he was the American Society of Plastic Surgeons Conference’s International Resident Academic Bowl Champion.

Dr. Linville and his staff have been noted for their professionalism, thoroughness, and kindness. Patients are consistently happy with their results, saying things like “I could not be happier with my experience,” “I have incredibly natural and beautiful results,” and “I cannot believe my results.”

To start your own transformation, schedule a consultation at Linville Plastic Surgery.
