Michael S. Lofgren, MD, FACS

Name: Michael S. Lofgren, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Michael S. Lofgren is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing at Plastic Surgery Center of Hampton Roads, in Williamsburg and Newport News, Virginia.

Dr. Lofgren specializes in tummy tucks, breast enhancement (augmentation, reduction, and lifts), Brazilian butt lifts, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers. He also offers a broad range of noninvasive or minimally invasive treatments, including dermal fillers, CoolSculping, Ultherapy skin tightening, laser skin resurfacing, and neuromodulators such as Botox.

“The chance to work with patients, identify their hopes and goals, and then see them through to the final result is what keeps me excited every day,” says Dr. Lofgren. “Techniques change rapidly, but the foundation of any amazing result will always be the relationship between the patient and surgeon.” He emphasizes the importance of open communication, as well as taking the time to truly understand his patients’ goals and develop customized treatment plans.

Dr. Lofgren earned his medical degree and completed a residency in General Surgery from Eastern Virginia Medical School in Norfolk. He went on to complete a fellowship in Plastic Surgery at the University of Miami in Miami, Florida, where trained with some of the top surgeons in his field.

Board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and the American Board of Surgery – General, Dr. Lofgren is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

Whether you’re seeking a full body makeover, noninvasive facial rejuvenation, or something in between, Dr. Lofgren and his staff in Williamsburg and Newport News, Virginia look forward to learning more about your aesthetic goals during a private consultation.

Peter J. Vonu, MD, FACS

Name: Peter J. Vonu, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Peter J. Vonu is an established and highly respected board-certified plastic surgeon serving the greater Newport News, Virginia area as a member of the experienced surgical team at Plastic Surgery Center of Hampton Roads.

With over 33 years in practice, Dr. Vonu specializes in plastic surgery of the breast, body, and face. He is particularly well-known for his excellence in breast enhancements, tummy tucks, facelifts, and rhinoplasty.

When asked why he chose plastic surgery as a specialty, Dr. Vonu explained, “It offers me the luxury of being both a physician and an artist. I enjoy developing a vision with my patient and then bringing it to fruition.” This collaborative approach is greatly appreciated by his patients, who enjoy excellent results that often exceed their expectations.

Dr. Vonu obtained his medical degree from the Medical College of Ohio in Toledo and completed a residency in Plastic Surgery at the University of Florida in Gainesville.

In addition to being board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Vonu is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), the Lipoplasty Society of North America, and the Medical Society of Virginia. He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

Dr. Vonu has been recognized in Hampton Roads Magazine’s Top Doc’s Hall of Fame in Plastic Surgery several times, and Virginia Living Magazine has also named him a Top Plastic Surgeon in Virginia.
