When can I get filler after surgery from broken femur?
At least 2 weeks
Jul 11, 2022
While the surgery and Lovenox are not absolute contraindications to treatment with Botox or fillers, you’ll likely want to wait at least 2 weeks after discontinuing the Lovenox to prevent excessive bruising or bleeding.
What is the best treatment for frown lines?
Neuromodulators for the win
Jul 8, 2022
Neuromodulators, like Dysport or Botox, can help soften those lines immensely by relaxing the frowning/furrowing muscles. When the lines are as deep as yours, neuromodulators alone may not completely get rid of them. Additional treatments such as microneedling, dermal fillers, or other resurfacing modalities may be necessary to more completely erase these lines.

Lip proportion – is the rule of thumb that the lower lip should be bigger than the upper lip?
Depends
May 2, 2022
For most Caucasian women, lips look the best when the ratio of top to bottom lip height is 1:1.618. This ratio can be closer to 1:1 in women of different ethnicities. It also depends on your goals! Keeping the ratios close to what is common for your ethnicity will help ensure the results look really natural.
Can I inject Botox just to frontalis?
Maybe
May 2, 2022
Treating just the frontalis is definitely an option, however since the frontalis and the glabella muscles are connected via fascia, treating just the frontalis can *sometimes* lead to more movement and wrinkles in the glabella area.

What are the benefits of PRF injections?
Results build slowly, but work!
Mar 21, 2022
PRF is a wonderful undereye treatment for a couple of reasons. One, it is very safe. Filler in the undereye area carries some risks that PRF does not have. Second, PRF contains growth factors that stimulate collagen that can improve that crepey appearance and somewhat improve the look of dark circles and hollowing. The results build slowly – you may start to see benefit at 2-3 months after your first treatment, then more results compound after subsequent treatments.
