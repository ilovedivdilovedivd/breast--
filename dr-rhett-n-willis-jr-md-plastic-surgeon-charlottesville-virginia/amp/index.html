What is the difference (if any) between Natrelle Soft Touch, Responsive and Cohesive implants?
Implant cohesivity
Aug 28, 2022
The difference in the three implants are increasing cohesivety of the gel. The most cohesive is usually reserved for breast reconstruction. The Response and Soft Touch are the softest and middle firmness, respectively. If you are going for the round “augmented look,” then the soft touch might be the better option. If you are looking for “natural results,” then the Response should work just fine. You should not experience much rippling with silicone gel.

Want multiple face and body procedures, can they be combined in one anesthesia?
Combining procedures
Aug 28, 2022
You should break up these up into two OR procedures. For safety purposes and surgeon fatigue, the face procedures could be performed in one setting, and the body procedures could be performed in a separate setting. I would not recommend getting all of these at the same time. Hope this helps
45 years old- Am I too old for a mommy makeover?
Age and MMO
Jul 30, 2022
No, you are not too old for a MMO. There is no age restriction and if this surgery will help bring back your pre-childbearing confidence, then you should go for it! Just discuss with your plastic surgeon any co-morbidities you may have so that you are safe for surgery. A BMI of 30 is fine to move forward with a MMO. Good Luck!

Is 6.5 hours for a mommy makeover the amount of time I will be under anesthesia?
MMO Anesthesia
Jul 30, 2022
A MMO can include many different procedures, so the estimated time will be determined by how much you are getting done. The core of a MMO is breast and tummy work, which should be at, or under 6 hours. If you are doing more procedures, and the time is estimated to be over 6 hours, then you should have this done in a hospital setting with the option for an overnight observation. Hope this helps.
