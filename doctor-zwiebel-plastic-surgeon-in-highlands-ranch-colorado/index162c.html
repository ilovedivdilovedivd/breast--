Getting an underboob tattoo done in June and a breast lift with implants in August. Is there a risk
Tattoo Near Breast Crease
May 22, 2018
Probably best to wait on the tattoo: breast augmentation does redrape the skin in the crease area.Depending on the size of the implant you are choosing and the tone of your skin, an inch of the skin below your current crease could end up as part of the lower curve of your breast.I think it best to wait on the tattoo, but if you can’t, at least discuss with your surgeon about the planned location of the tattoo.
Gurgling noise in my breast 5 weeks post op: I still hear and feel it very frequently.
Gurgling Noise after Breast Augmentation
May 21, 2018
Hearing squeaking or gurgling for days or weeks after breast augmentation is common.Both air and fluid will be in the pocket around the implant after the procedure, but will be absorbed gradually.If the sounds or sensatins persist beyond 2-3 months, consult with your surgeon.
Can 36HH be lifted with implants and without a reduction?
Implants Don’t Lift Breasts
