Breast augmentation surgery in Birmingham AL is performed mostly for aesthetic reasons and applies to:
– To increase the volume and improve the shape of the breast;
– To restore breast volume after pregnancy or after a big weight loss;
– Breast asymmetry, congenital malformations;
– Mammoplasty used as a method of correction after undergoing breast surgery, such as after a mastectomy for cancer or resections for benign tumors, the transferred inflammatory diseases (mastitis);
– To correct the breast ptosis, as in an isolated form, or in combination with the breast lift;

Silicone implants used for breast augmentation. Currently, it is uncontested, the safest and most effective way.

Surgery Breast Implants Alabama Is Performed Under General Anesthesia

Implants are round and teardrop shaped. Their shell is made of non-reactive, non-polluting material. The textured surface reduces the likelihood of fibrosis is a hardening and compacting the breast. The gel inside gives the natural softness and does not flow even in case of damage of the outer shell of the implant.

Surgical access or incision position for install implants is usually chosen on the basis of objective parameters and the patient’s wishes. The most commonly used:

  1. inframammary access;
  2. periareolar access;
  3. transaxillary access.

Silicone And Saline Breast Implants In Birmingham, AL

Transumbilical access (through the navel) is also used, but much less than other. There are several options for implant placement: under the breast, under the fascia under the pectoralis major muscle and dual plane breast augmentation, the upper part under the muscle, the lower part under the breast. When the thickness of the covering tissue is less than 1 cm in all cases implant should be placed beneath the muscle.

Breast augmentation by inframammary access (incision under your breast). The most widely used method of breast enlargement. An incision made a length of 3-4 cm in the crease under the breast. Through this incision form a pocket under the breast or under the pectoralis major muscle. The implant installed to the formed pocket.

The pocket is sewn up. The advantage of this access is its simplicity, less pain and extent of swelling in the postoperative period. The main disadvantage it is not cosmetic type of access. The scar can be formed atrophic, extended , also characterized by the migration of the scar in the long-term period (2 years).

Breast augmentation through the periareolar access (an incision made on the edge of the areola ). This is one of the most inconspicuous incisions used for breast augmentation. An incision made near the nipple, on the pigmented skin of the areola.

Through this incision is opened a pocket under the breast or under the pectoralis major muscle. The implant is placed in a pocket formed. The surgeon closes the incisions with layered sutures (stitches).

The advantage of this access for breast augmentation is the scar less noticeable after surgery in Birmingham, Alabama. A round implant and the implant an anatomical shape can be installed through periareolar access.

The disadvantage is the difficulty of the installation at a very small size of the areola. Contrary to idle speculation, the breast tissue is not incise and the ductal system is not broken in this method.

Breast augmentation through the transaxillary access (an incision made to the armpit). The incision made in the armpit in the area of hair growth. Then, the surgeon forms an a pocket through this incision (either under the pectoralis major muscle or under the breast).

Formation of the pocket through the transaxillary access is a technically more difficult than the inframammary and periareolar access, because in this position is difficult to reach the bottom of the chest.

It is also difficult to inspect the pocket, in order to determine whether there are small blood vessels that need to cauterize or scar tissue that must be removed surgically. Therefore, in most cases, for the formation of pocket used an endoscopic equipment. It is a special tools with long handles and a video camera with a illuminator. It is possible to better see and operate inside the breast by using fiber-optic instruments connected to television screen. The implant placed in a pocket formed and sutured.

The advantage of transaxillary access is no visible scar on your breast. The disadvantage is a technical complexity and the need to use special equipment (an endoscope).
