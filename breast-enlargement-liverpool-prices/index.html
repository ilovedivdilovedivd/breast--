Procedure For Breast Augmentation Involves Surgically Inserting Breast Implants Underneath The Natural Tissue

To perform breast augmentation in aesthetic medicine clinic in Liverpool England the doctors use only high-quality implants. Breast Implants are in two types, round and anatomical shaped.

The choice of implant depends on the structure of the body, the physiological characteristics of the organism and desires of the patient. All questions on the choice of implant placement and a surgical approach discussed with the doctor during the consultation.

Indications and contraindications for breast augmentation surgery.

In most cases, this procedure is performed in the following indications:

  1. The breast is too small or asymmetrical.
  2. A breasts ptosis.
  3. The breast is not proportional to the body.

As with any surgery, breast surgery has its contraindications:

  1. Diabetes;
  2. Disorders of blood clotting;
  3. Acute and chronic forms of diseases.
  4. The predisposition to the formation of keloid scars.
  5. Mental illness.

How is breast augmentation surgery performed?

Breast implants are installed through a very small incision of 3-4 cm., and the seams are imposed by a special method of “invisible seam”.

Thus, scar resorbed quickly, and then it becomes almost imperceptible.

Three variants of the surgical approach are used:

  1. On the lower edge of the areola,
  2. Through the crease under the breast,
  3. Through the axilla.

The choice of location of the future incision is performed by the surgeon based on the patient’s body and taking into account their wishes and an incision made so that the postoperative scar was imperceptible.
