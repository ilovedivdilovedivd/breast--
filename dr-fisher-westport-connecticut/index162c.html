Which filler would you recommend for cheek lines?
HA Filler to Start
Sep 13, 2021
Hello,
I would recommend starting with a Hyaluronic Acid (HA) filler such as Juvéderm or Restylane. The benefit of these fillers is that they can be dissolved if for whatever reason you do not like the result. My recommendation is to always start slow with a small amount and add more as you progress. The main thing to avoid in this area is looking overfilled.
