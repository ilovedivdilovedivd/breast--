Variants of incision for installation implants.

During the breast augmentation incision is usually done in one of 3 areas: around the nipple (periareolar access), in the crease under the breast (inframammary access) or in the armpit (transaxillary access).

Typically, the incision is performed on 1st of three schemes for such operations:

Periareolar access an incision made around the nipple. The advantage of this access is the maximum ability to hide the incision site. But there is a risk of loss of nipple sensitivity.

Transaxillary access an incision made to the armpit. The place incision will be more prominent. This method is used when the patient does not wish to have any scarring in the area of the mammary glands.

Inframammary access: an incision made under the breast. This is a very popular option, since incision line is located in a natural crease of your breast and almost invisible. The plastic surgeon of our clinic more detail will tell you about the features of each access, will help you choose the most suitable option implant before breast enlargement.

Placement of implants.

Plastic Breast Surgery Lasts One To Two Hours And Is Typically Performed With General Anesthesia

Methods for installing the implants are as follows:

– Subglandular: the implant placement under the breasts on top of the muscle layer. The duration of the augmentation mammoplasty and the recovery period is a minimum. The edges of implant will be visible under the skin if you select the wrong implant size. Also, when such placement will difficult to visualize breast tissue on a mammogram.

– Submuscular: the breast implant is install beneath the pectoralis major muscle, without releasing the inferior origin of the muscle proper. In this case the possibility of probing the implant through the skin is reduced, the risk of compaction around the scar tissue is decreases. Also, this method provides a better picture of breast tissue on a mammogram. The disadvantage is the long duration of the breast enhancement and the recovery period.

You can discuss with the doctor, which of the above methods will contribute to the achievement of specific your goals, and together choose the method that suits you.
