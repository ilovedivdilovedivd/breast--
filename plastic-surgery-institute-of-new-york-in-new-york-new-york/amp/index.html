Doctors

Adam Schaffner, MD, FACS

Name: Adam Schaffner, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Adam Schaffner, is a triple board-certified, double fellowship-trained plastic surgeon practicing in New York City. Known for his impeccable skills and artistry in plastic and reconstructive surgery for the face, breast, and body, Dr. Schaffner specializes in breast enhancement procedures such as breast augmentation, breast lifts, breast reductions, breast reconstruction, and revision breast surgery, as well as tummy tucks (also known as abdominoplasty), butt augmentation (also known as Brazilian butt lift or BBL), precision liposuction with BodyTite, liposculputing with fat transfer breast augmentation, and complete mommy makeovers.

At his boutique private practice in midtown Manhattan just off of Park Avenue, the Plastic Surgery Institute of New York, Dr. Schaffner also performs facelifts, neck lifts, eyelid surgery, brow lifts, and submental liposuction (aka chin lipo) with FaceTite. He also offers a variety of minimally invasive treatments, including injectables such as BOTOX, Dysport, Jeuveau, Xeomin, and dermal fillers like Juvéderm, Restylane, Sculptra, RHA, Bellafill, Radiesse, BELOTERO, and more.

People visit Dr. Schaffner because they know that they’ll receive quality care and beautiful results. “Choosing the right plastic surgeon is the most important decision you will make” in getting a result you’ll be thrilled with, says Dr. Schaffner. “I personally meet with each of my patients to develop an individualized plan. We will review everything you need to know to be informed, prepared, and feel comfortable.”

After completing his undergraduate education at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia, Dr. Schaffner obtained his medical degree from Rush Medical College in Chicago. He then completed a plastic and reconstructive surgery residency at Detroit Medical Center at the Wayne State University School of Medicine, followed by an otolaryngology – head and neck surgery residency at Stony Brook University Medical Center.

Dr. Schaffner honed his skills while training in a breast and aesthetic plastic surgery fellowship at Georgetown University Hospital in Washington DC and an advanced facial plastic and reconstructive surgery fellowship, during which he was a member of the medical staff at Stanford Hospital and Clinics.

Dr. Schaffner is a diplomat of (certified by) the American Board of Plastic Surgery, the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery.

A member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and The Aesthetic Society, Dr. Schaffner is also a Fellow of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American College of Surgeons.

Highly respected by his peers and colleagues, Dr. Schaffner has been honored with numerous awards and accolades, such as being named a Top Doctor by Castle Connolly and included in the Consumers’ Research Council of America’s Guide to America’s Top Plastic Surgeons. He has received the Patients’ Choice Award and Most Compassionate Doctor Award and selected as a New York Super Doctor.

Dr. Schaffner has also been featured by various media outlets and publications, including Good Morning America, The New York Times, CBS News, Telemundo, USA TODAY, and Newsweek, among others.
