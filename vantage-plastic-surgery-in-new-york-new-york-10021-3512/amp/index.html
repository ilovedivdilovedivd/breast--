Statement: Dr. Aleksandr Shteynberg is a double board-certified plastic surgeon specializing in reconstructive and cosmetic surgery. Dr. Shteynberg has always been drawn to surgery. As an undergraduate, he attended New York University with a pre-med track and graduated with Cum Laude. He completed his medical and surgical training at SUNY Downstate Medical Center. Following the residency, Dr. Shteynberg trained at Long Island Plastic Surgery Group in plastic and reconstructive surgery and served as chief resident.
