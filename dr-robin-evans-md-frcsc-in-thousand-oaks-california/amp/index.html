Do I have a chance doing an elective surgery being on Eliquis (blood thinners)?
Blood thinners and breast augmentation
Feb 19, 2019
Hi Neat7007,
Breast augmentation is an elective procedure and as such the level of risk should be very low. Hematoma is a very real possible complication in individuals not on blood thinners. As such, it would be rare to find a surgeon who would be willing to perform the procedure with excessive additional risks.
Hope this helps!
