Loose inner thigh skin typically does not change after a tummy tuck and will need to be addressed as a separate area. As for performing everything at the same time versus in a staged fashion, it will depend on your medical history and the extent of surgery required in each area. I have indentations, spider veins and hyperpigmentation after my augmentation. What can be done for this?
Conservative management for now
Aug 9, 2023
Scars typically continue to fade up until 1 year after surgery, so do not lose hope! You can try silicone gel or silicone sheeting over the scar in the meantime. Also make sure you apply SPF over the scar when you are in the sun.
9 month post op breast augmentation. My left breast looks like it has more upper pole fullness
Pocket malposition or capsular contracture
Aug 9, 2023
The left implant appears to be higher. This could either be the result of pocket malposition or some degree of capsular contracture on the left. Either may require surgical revision. Oftentimes it is prudent to wait until 12 months from surgery to see how the pockets ultimately settle, but at 9 months out it is not unreasonable to start have discussions regarding your concerns.

Want to explant 420cc, under muscle saline implants – do I also need a lift?
Consider deflating implants in clinic
Jul 31, 2023
Hi there. You will have some sagging with explantation only. Whether it is to a degree that is acceptable to you, that is dependent on your expectations. Since you have saline implants, an option is to have your implants deflated in clinic, and allow your breast several weeks to settle. After that, you can assess how you feel about the appearance of your breasts and will have a better idea of whether you want to proceed with explantation only or explantation with lift.
