Breast Enlargement Dublin Prices

Breast augmentation in Dublin is performed for women who desire a fuller, more attractive figure.

Currently, the breast surgery or correction of its forms is carried out as often as removing inflamed appendicitis.

Today, plastic breast surgery in demand not only among the pop stars or top models, but among ordinary women who dream to be beautiful, and to have an attractive body shape.

All this has led to the fact that in Germany, where plastic surgery is a leader in the world, each year 1 million surgeries performed for cosmetic purposes. Breast augmentation (Mammoplasty) in Dublin is the usual and very common. For example, a leading specialist of the Center for Plastic Surgery performs annually more than 1,000 operations for changing the shape of the breast.

Modern methods makes breast augmentation a practically safest kind of surgical intervention: for a long time implants perform their function, the rehabilitation period is about 1-2 weeks.

Mammoplasty are often prescribed in the case the breast ptosis. Quite a number of women feel diffident due to the sagging of the breast, that causes them to have recourse to plastic surgery. The most commonly breast ptosis develops due to age-related decrease in skin elasticity, a sharp weight loss and breastfeeding.

Although at times you can hear about the conservative ways of correction of breast shape, but in reality analogue of breast surgery by efficiency simply does not exist.

The cost of the breast augmentation

The price of plastic surgery on the breast is enough constant, but can oscillate depending on the complexity and objectives of the forthcoming surgery. For example, the price for breast augmentation with and without implants will differ.

It also the cost of the breast enlargement includes: costs associated with the stay in the hospital for 1-2 days: full board (payment by DRG), the surgeon’s fee, the cost of preparation for breast implants surgery and anesthesia.

The price of plastic surgery on a breast also depends on the cost of post-operative treatment, if there will be unforeseen complications (as a rule, this component of the price is completely impossible to predict in advance).

How to live with the “new” breasts?

Leading experts warn that in the first week after breast enlargement procedure can be painful sensations, weakness, swelling of the breast tissue and increasing its sensitivity.

By the end of the first month after bust enlargement these phenomena almost completely disappear.

In addition, many patients in the case of breast enlargement marks an excessive tension of the skin at the first time, as the body needs time to get used to the new forms.
