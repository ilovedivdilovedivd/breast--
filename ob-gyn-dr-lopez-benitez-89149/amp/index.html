Can the Geneveve procedure be done after recent hysterectomy?
Geneveve after a hysterectomy
Dec 30, 2018
If it has been 12 weeks after the hysterectomy there shouldn’t be a problem at all. The area treated by the Geneveve is the outer third of the vagina. The innermost area of the vagina (where the cervix was and now there are stitches) is not treated or even touched while doing this procedure.

Would Percocet interfere with the O-Shot?
Narcotics and O-Shot
Sep 29, 2018
There shouldn’t be any problem by taking Percocet or any narcotics after the O-Shot. We prefer that the patient avoids any NSAIDs close to the administration of the O-Shot to prevent a decrease in the effectiveness of the treatment. Narcotics (like Percocet) don’t have NSAIDs.
