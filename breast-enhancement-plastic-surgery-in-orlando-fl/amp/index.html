Breast Enhancement Plastic Surgery In Orlando, FL

Mammoplasty (breast augmentation) in Orlando, FL is cosmetic surgery aimed at correcting the form of mammary glands for aesthetic purposes.

There are various options of mammoplasty aimed both to increase mammary gland forms, such as by installing silicone implants, and for breast reduction (reduction mammoplasty).

Breast lift surgery (mastopexy)

Mastopexy is an operation aimed at correcting shape of the breast by a movement of the nipple-areola complex to the new prepared place. Before the breast lift, you must also pass tests, inspection, to perform measurements of the breast, with a plastic surgeon discuss the patient’s wishes in order to achieve the required parameters.

Silicone Breast Implants is one of the most common cosmetic procedures performed today in Florida

Before breast lift or augmentation surgery, the patient should buy a sports bra or surgical-bra for secure customized fit of breast in the postoperative period. The surgeon performs marking of provisions of the nipple-areola complex in over both breasts in the form of “keyhole”.

Duration of surgery up to 3 hours. Breast lift is performed under general anesthesia by experienced brigade of surgeons, anesthesiologist, middle and junior medical staff. Terms of hospital stay up to 3 days, then the patient comes to the hospital on the dressings.

Mastopexy is an effective way to eliminate drooping breast after lactation and ptosis. The surgical access includes periareolar approach, vertical horizontal periareolar, incisions are sutured fiberwise with absorbable suture PDSII or its analogues.

Scar After Breast Implants In Orlando, FL

After surgery, of the skin is a T-shaped scar extending from the the nipple-areola complex to the crease under the breast. The skin is sutured non-absorbable atraumatic suture material an intradermal cosmetic suture.

Contraindications are the following conditions and diseases:

  1. Diabetes in a stage of decompensation and subcompensation;
  2. Oncological diseases;
  3. Chronic and acute infectious diseases;

  6. Mental diseases;
  7. Lactation;
  8. The tendency to develop keloid scars.

Reduction mammoplasty

Reduction mammoplasty is the plastic surgery procedure for reducing the size breast. During surgery excised the excess breast tissue, skin, and transposes the nipple-areola complex (NAC) to a new location, leaving the mammary gland is reduced in volume. Breast reduction occurs according to the same basic principle as the mastopexy. Preparing the patient for surgery and contraindications are analogous as to breast lift (mastopexy).

Orlando Breast Enlargement Reoperation

Breast augmentation surgery in Orlando is scientifically known as endoprosthesis of mammary glands placement. Breast surgery is one of the most widely sought-after a plastic surgery for the correction of breasts in women.

The boob augmentation is to install a silicone implant through a small surgical access in the area areola (periareolar, transaxillary), axillary area (axillary) or Inframammary (natural crease under your breast), which is then sutured cosmetic intracutaneous seam. Implants are used well-known manufacturers.

Selection of the implant is carried, together with the surgeon. All implants have a three-layer shell: the outer shell of the implant made of silicone and it can be smooth, roughened, textured. Inside it is a high cohesive gel. The implant may be round or anatomical shape. Silicone breast implants have a different profile, which can be low, medium, high and ultra-high, as well as the height, width and volume.
