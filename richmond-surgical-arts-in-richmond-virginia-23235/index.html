Breast augmentation results are uneven - is it fair to be disappointed?
Uneven results after breast augmentation
Nov 13, 2023
It is great to see two sets of post op pictures since you have improved over time. The right breast implant pocket seems a bit low to start and the left too high. At this point in time if you like the left side better then your doctor could raise the right breast fold. I hope this helps, Good Luck!
What can I do about implants that are too big for my frame?
Implants too big
Nov 13, 2023
Replacing with smaller implants is possible. You have likely good skin elasticity and going down to 350cc should work out fine. Please ask you plastic surgeon since you may need some of the lateral pocket tightened. Good Luck!
Mons pubis too high after tummy tuck - advice?
High scar after tummy tuck
Aug 10, 2023
You may have had not enough skin for a traditional tummy tuck that keeps the scar about 7-8 cv above the top edge of the vulvar commissure. The two options then are to move the scar higher or to create a midline vertical scar which does have a higher healing problem but is what I prefer to do since then all the scar is hidden by a bathing suit except 1-2 inches coming straight up the middle which is not much of a cosmetic concern. It is unlikely that you have enough laxity to move your scar...
