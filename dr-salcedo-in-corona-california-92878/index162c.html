Moisturizer with Retin-A – Apply Which First?
Moisturizer with Retin-A
Nov 28, 2018
You can AND should use moisturizer when undergoing Retin-A treatment. I use 0.1% tretinoin aka Retin-A myself (Obagi). I like the Obagi Hydrate moisturizer and my usual sequence is to apply the Retin-A on a clean face wait a few minutes then apply the moisturizer. I agree with others who say you shouldn’t mix the Retin-A with the moisturizer at the same time as this may cause you to use less Retin-A, effectively diluting it.
