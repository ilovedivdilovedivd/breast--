Modern plastic surgery has at its disposal a number of methods for breast augmentation. They differ in the degree of invasiveness and term conservation proof effect.

In clinics in Pittsburgh, PA the breast augmentation is carried out with the use of silicone implants last generation, as well as injectable methods – using lipofilling and biodegradable gel (type Macrolane).

There are the following approaches breast augmentation with implants:

1) Through inframammary access (in the crease under the breast);
2) A periareolar access ( along the periphery of the areola);
3) An endoscopic method (Transaxillary access);
4) The operation in two stages using dermotension expander;
5) The method of breast augmentation through the navel.
