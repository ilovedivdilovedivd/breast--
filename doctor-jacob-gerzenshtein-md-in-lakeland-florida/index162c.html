Can I use my own fat to fix tuberous breasts?
In mild cases only
Nov 3, 2019
There are different degrees of breast constriction/herniation and so whether the problem may be corrected via fat only depends on how severe your case is. In the more severe cases, fat injection only will not address the problem as the breast skin that is protruded would be more likely to expand, compounding the problem. Initially the breast would need to be reshaped, following this, fat grafting would be used to augment volume.
