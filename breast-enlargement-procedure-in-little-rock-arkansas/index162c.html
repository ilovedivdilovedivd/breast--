Types of surgical access and placement of implants for breast augmentation

An important step in preparation for any breast enhancement is the choice of surgical approach, or in other words – the incision. Especially it concerns the plastic surgery, and breast enlargement in particular.

Place of incision depends on the characteristics of each augmentation mammoplasty and selected in each case separately. A very important point is a hide the scar maximally.

Therefore frequently an incision is made in the natural folds, through which you can easily hide the scar. It is also necessary to take care not only about the aesthetic beauty, but also about its physiological integrity to preserve the natural activity of the body as the exocrine secretion organ.

Incision made in the wrong place can lead to overlapping of the mammary glands and the development of mastitis or cysts. Therefore, the incisions for breast augmentation performed in one of the three most popular areas:

1. An incision in the crease under the breast is the most simple and some surgeons use quite often. The length of the incision varies from 3 to 5 centimeters, the implant is placed under the pectoralis major muscle.

There is a variant of the implant placement completely under the muscle that makes it possible to completely cover the implant of the muscle tissue. This method eliminates any possible negative consequences with respect to the lower muscle twitches or effect of the “wash board”. The disadvantage of this method is quite noticeable scar after bust enlargement, so this method can be used subject to available indications.

2. The incision along the edge of the areola is quite common method among many surgeons. This method is especially good if you have a small ptosis (sagging) breasts, when you needs a breast lift. In such cases, the periareolar incision is made. Breast augmentation is performed in conjunction with a breast lift. Placed implant is not completely in the muscular pocket is a combined method of installation, when the upper pole of the implant is under the breast, and the bottom pole – under the muscle. The incision along the edge of the areola provides a practically undetectable scar after bust enhancement.

3. The incision in the armpit can be considered the most inconspicuous, but also more complicated in terms of implementation. This method is quite attractive for the patient, as the scar on a visible part of the body is virtually absent. However, not all surgeons are skilled in the use of this method, and it requires certain professional skills.

4. Surgical access to the navel is the least prevalent method. However, when using this method it is possible to install only the empty breast implants to the implant pockets, the plastic surgeon then filled each device with saline solution, which are used very rarely.

What kind of breast implants are there?

Little Rock, Arkansas Plastic Surgery Breast Augmentation

Modern types of implants for breast augmentation can be divided on several parameters:

1. The shape of breast implants. Today, there are two forms – round and anatomical (teardrop). The round shape has been used earlier in the 90s and gave a good result, but did not retain the natural shape of the breast. Now more commonly used anatomical or teardrop shape, which received its name from the similarity of form with natural breasts. Profile implant is chosen individually. High implants often installed under the pectoralis major muscle. Ultra high implants have a high density and are installed subcutaneously, breast or pectoralis muscle and fascial pocket.

2. Filler: saline solution, silicone gel. Do not focus on the price of the implant. It is better to choose the most suitable for you. When saline implant ruptures, leaks, and empties, it quickly deflates, and thus can be readily surgically removed. Silicone gels are of different viscosities, and more natural feel quality transfer, and also do not flow in the case of small ruptures.

3. The shell of breast implants. In the last century it was practiced by direct injection of the gel into the cavity of the muscle tissue, leading to unfortunate consequences, because now the use of non-coated gel is strictly prohibited.

Now the most popular are breast implants roughened (textured) surface, which allows them, in contrast to the smooth implants is grow into the tissue and does not be displaced forming folds and wrinkles. The volume of the implants depends on the patient’s wishes. Often, the volume of breast implants ranges from 200 to 700 cm3, but the most commonly used implant volume of 300-450 cm3.

The main fear is the safety of implants. Therefore, the choice must take into account all the parameters and characteristics, as well as the use of implants popular manufacturers with a worldwide reputation that give on its products a lifelong guarantee.
