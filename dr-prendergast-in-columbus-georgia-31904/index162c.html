Would I be a good candidate for a panniculectomy?
Panniculectomy or Not
May 14, 2019
Thank you for your question. Based on your issues, you will be a good candidate for removal of the excess skin. This skin can be addressed with either a panniculectomy or abdominoplasty (tummy tuck). A panniculectomy will just remove the excess skin, the abdominoplasty will also tighten the rectus muscles (abdominal muscles) which will help to give you a flatter abdomen after surgery. Please seek out a board certified plastic surgeon. Good Luck.
