Types of breast implants

Best Breast Augmentation In Lafayette Louisiana Can Restore A More Youthful Form

One of the popular ways to increase the breast is to use implants. There are 3 types of implants: silicone or a biocompatible gel or saline fillers. Plastic surgeons are very fond of silicone implants and work well with them.

They may be round or conform anatomical shape of the breast, as well as smooth and textured. Implants filled with saline solution, can be placed in the breast tissue using a small incision.

However, the breast shape with the installation of this implant may look less natural.

Bio-gel implants are able resorbed, if for whatever reason their shell be damaged. Are thus formed non-hazardous substances for the female body : glucose, carbon dioxide and water.

Silicone implant in case of damage can be removed by surgery.

How is located an implant for breast augmentation:

  1. the implant is installed under the pectoralis major muscle;
  2. the implant is installed just under the breast.

There is a third method of implant placement, when the upper pole of the implant is placed under the pectoral muscle and the lower pole – just under the breast. This location is used in cases where lower bound of fastening of the pectoralis major muscle is much higher than the lower the breast border. The fourth method, which is used relatively rarely – placing the implant directly underneath the fascia of the pectoralis major muscle.

Each of the methods of implant placement has its “pros” and “cons.” Only a plastic surgeon can accurately determine which method is best suited to the particular case, at the time of examination of the patient. In this case the surgeon must take into account the overall state of the mammary glands, their look, especially the anatomical structure and so on.

Placement of the implant under the pectoralis major muscle

The implant is placed a completely under the pectoralis major muscle in the case where there is maximum convergence of the lower boundaries of the pectoralis major muscle and breast. In this situation, you can achieve the most natural results.

If your breast is very sags down, concurrently it is necessary to perform a breast lift. After that, the breast becomes naturally beautiful shape.

So, when placing the implant under the pectoralis major muscle, a plastic surgeon achieves a natural and beautiful form a mammary gland, since the contours of the implant in this case, the maximum smoothed out by muscle, as well as creates a sense of fullness of the upper pole the breast.

However, if you decide install implant in this manner, it must be remembered that in comparison with other variants of its location the healing passes more longer, traumatization breast tissue and the risk of complications increases , but the risk of fibrosis is reduced.
