How soon after dissolving lip filler can you refill?
Dissolving filler
Jun 11, 2023
Hi and thanks for your question! It is best to wait 2 weeks after dissolving filler because you want to give time to see a final result before filling again. The face always swells with any injection, and waiting 2 weeks is a safe amount of time to have a stable starting point for refilling. Best of luck!
Would you call this a botched lip job?
Lip filler
Jun 11, 2023
Hi and thanks for your question! Only you can say if you are happy with your result or not. It’s hard to say from the pictures, but there may be some uneven areas or even over-filling. If you are unhappy you can have them dissolved with hyaluronidase, an enzyme that breaks up hyaluronic acid fillers. It may take several times of injecting that enzyme to get you where you want to be. Best of luck!
Allergic reaction to fillers. If I am allergic to one brand, would I be allergic to all?
Lip filler and Blisters
Jun 11, 2023
Hi and thanks for your question. It’s still possible that you had a cold sore even with a negative culture. An allergy to the filler is also a possibility. In that case, you can dissolve the filler with hyaluronidase, use steroids as you did, and benardryl. If you had an allergy, I would find a provider who can do testing or spot testing to see if another brand may work better for you. Best of luck!
