Rippling despite implant revision
Breast reconstruction revision/ enhancement for rippling after mastectomy with reconstruction
Oct 31, 2023
Rippling is common after breast cancer reconstruction revision. While changing to a higher cohesive gel is one way to improve, sometimes pocket size modifications to make a better “hand in glove” fit between the pocket and the implant and fat grafting can also help. Liposuction can be taking from a variety of different areas, especially in thin women, to avoid contour changes to any one area and can also be combined with ‘off the shelf’ fat products to expand the volume of available fat.
En bloc explant with lift 1 year ago gone wrong, why are my breasts deformed?
Breast deformities after en bloc capsulectomy
Oct 19, 2023
Unfortunately significant deformity after en bloc capsulectomy is not uncommon. The combination of fat transfer to fill some of the contour changes and a breast lift to address excess skin can help improve contour changes. Fat for transfer is usually removed using liposuction from stubborn areas and transferred to the breasts for natural volume.
What type of breast lift would be most effective/ get the best results?
Breast lift and adding volume without using an implant
Oct 14, 2023
The pattern of breast lift with the most control over final shape is an anchor, or wise pattern breast lift. For women not interested in implants, a breast lift with fat transfer can be a great option for adding some natural volume. Fat is removed from stubborn areas using liposuction techniques and transferred to the breast for custom cleavage lines.
