Doctors

George Lefkovits, MD

Name: George Lefkovits, MD
Statement: Dr. George Lefkovits is a double board-certified plastic surgeon and director of Park Plaza Plastic Surgery on New York City’s Upper East Side. With over 40 years experience, he is a recognized leader in women’s and men’s cosmetic surgery.

“I put my training and experience at the service of my patients,” says Dr. Lefkovitz. “I always emphasize creating a natural result.”

His many specialties include breast augmentation, Brazilian Butt Lift, tummy tuck, mommy makeovers, and gynecomastia surgery (male breast reduction).

Dr. Lefkovits attended Georgetown University School of Medicine in Washington D.C. and earned his medical degree from the State University of New York Downstate Medical College in Brooklyn, New York.

He went on to complete an internship in General Surgery at Long Island Jewish Medical Center in Queens, New York, followed by a residency in plastic surgery at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan, where he became chief resident.

Dr. Lefkovits is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery. He is a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

Committed to the education of future generations of plastic surgeons, Dr. Lefkovits serves as the guiding plastic surgery councilor for medical students at Downstate Medical College and a member of the plastic surgery teaching program at the Lenox Hill Hospital. He has also worked as the assistant professor of plastic surgery at New York Medical College. Respected within the international plastic surgery community, he lectures across the globe and once served as a guest plastic surgeon in the United Arab Emirates.

Dr. Lefkovits has been featured in a wide variety of media outlets including Good Day New York, The Baltimore Sun, the San Antonio Express, World Models Magazine, Metrosource Magazine, and Telemundo TV, as well as television specials on NBC and Fox.
