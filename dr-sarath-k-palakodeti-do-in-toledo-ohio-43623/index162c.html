Does Botox work on saggy breasts?
Botox cannot be used to lift breasts
Jul 10, 2015
Thanks for the question. Botox is an injectable toxin that’s injected into muscles to relax/paralyze them. Botox has many uses but is not used to lift breasts. Only surgery can be used to lift/reduce breast tissue. If you are truly having medical issues such as back and neck pain a breast reduction may be able to be performed. In general you should let your breasts fully develop before deciding to undergo any type of surgery.
