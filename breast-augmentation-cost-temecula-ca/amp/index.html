Breast Job Cost Temecula CA

Breast augmentation In Temecula, CA is a very commonly performed plastic surgical procedure that can enhance the body contour of women who feel that their breast size is too small or have lost breast volume due to weight loss or following pregnancy. Many of the fair sex are sure that the “right” size of the breast is the key to success in career, society and in personal life. They are right in part: a beautiful breast gives a woman confidence, good mood and general state.

If nature has not endowed you magnificent bust, and you think this is a real problem, then the solution of this problem is breast augmentation or mammoplasty. Women is а frighten stories about the inevitable complications after surgery, many people doubt – whether to make a step towards the dream. Here we will try to dispel all your fears.

Complications after mammoplasty were frequent in the 90s. Then for breast augmentation was used a polyacrylamide gel. It is were injected directly between the pectoral muscle and the mammary gland, eventually began to migrate or cause inflammation. At the moment its use in plastic surgery banned worldwide.

Scientific studies have found no association between the occurrence of cancer and mammoplasty. This rumor was born, when the production of silicone implants has not yet reached the required level of quality.

Today, special safe implants are used for breast augmentation In Temecula. The implants consist of a silicone shell filled with a dense cohesive gel that reduces the risk of breaking and leaking to a minimum. Silicone implants are filled with gel (liquid or cohesive gel) or 9% saline solution. Each filler has its advantages. Leakage of saline at rupture the shell is absolutely safe for the body, but the breast becomes empty.

When a silicone breast implant ruptures it usually does not deflate.

Some patients complain that implants filled with salt water, can gurgles when walking and forms bumps and furrows (as a liquid gel). The implants with cohesive gel are most suitable on all parameters (perfectly keep their shape, do not form bumps), but they are more expensive.

The surgery is performed under general anesthesia, it lasts no more than 1.5 hours. The implant is installed between the breast and pectoralis major muscle or behind it. The incision is made around the nipple areola, in the crease under the breast, in the armpit, to as possible to hide scars.

The surgeon selects the method of access the placement of implant, depending on the individual characteristics of the patient. Pain disappear within 2-4 days. The inconvenience is the need to wear a compression underwear for months.

Breast augmentation surgery In Temecula is now one of the most popular and safest in the world. You can safely trust our professionals plastic surgery clinics, and become the owner of the attractive bust, forgetting all about the complexes and problems!
