Plastic Surgery Breast Enlargement in Nashville, Tennessee

Breast augmentation in Nashville TN is the enlargement of your breasts with help of silicone or saline implants, or your fat. Beautiful, well-contoured breasts at all times was considered a sign of perfection, femininity and sexuality. Currently, the only reliable and safe way to increase breast or change its shape is an aesthetic mammoplasty with implants installation.

Indications:

1. the post-lactation involution of mammary glands (as a result of breast-feeding);
2. reconstruction of the breast following a mastectomy or injury;
3. congenital malformations of the mammary glands (breast reduced, asymmetry, ptosis, itd);

Contraindications:

  1. Malignant breast tumors;
  2. Acute inflammatory disease (local and general);
  3. Severe somatic diseases (cardiovascular disease, diabetes, blood diseases);
  4. Progressing benign tumors of the mammary glands;
  5. Lack of skin and soft tissue to cover the implant;
  6. irradiation, ulcers, insufficient blood supply to the wound after breast surgery;

Breast Enhancing Nashville To Correct Asymmetry

Breast plastic surgery is performed under general anesthesia with mechanical ventilation of lungs. The duration of the bust enlargement from 1 to 2 hours. We use products of companies McGhan Mentor, Eurosilicon. Breast Implants of these brands have a three-layer protective shell and gel increased cohesiveness (“non-flowing”). Companies provide a certificate for a lifetime warranty on the product.

There are several approaches to the install breast implant: to the armpit, along the periphery of the areola and to the inframammary fold and the incision is at the umbilicus (navel).

When the access under the breast incision length usually ranges from 3 to 5 centimeters. The scar is almost invisible. This is the most secure and low-traumatic method and we give it priority. The incision along the lower edge of the areola is unvisible, but to install the implant through it much more difficult.

The breast tissue can be damaged when the pocket formed for implants. This is undesirable for women planning pregnancy and lactation. A little size of areola is a restrictions on the use of this access. There is a high risk of reducing sensitivity of the nipple and areola after breast job.

Breast augmentation in Nashville, Tennessee through an incision in the armpit is performed using endoscopic equipment. This access is used only for installation of round implants. Accordingly, the scar is virtually invisible. Breast augmentation with operative access to the top of the navel is a rarely used method.

Endoscopic technique is used in this case. Implants can be installed only with saline. The disadvantage is the high risk of asymmetric implants. During the breast enlargement procedure in the space of a mammary gland is formed “pocket” in which the implant is placed, and the wound is sutured in layers.
