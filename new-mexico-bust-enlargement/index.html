New Mexico Bust Enlargement

Beautiful breasts has always been a woman’s pride and an object of adoration of men and not only a symbol of feminine beauty and motherhood.

However, natural breast does not always have a beautiful shape and desired volume.

Augmentation mammoplasty in New Mexico can help you in solving this problem.

The operation is performed in order to increase mammary glands in the following indications:

a) Breast reduction in pregnancy and lactation;
b) hypoplasia of the mammary glands;
c) Physiological features (pronounced breast asymmetry, etc.).

Breast Implants Santa Fe 9 Days Post Op

The surgeon selects the type of implant, the volume and shape, method of installation and access on a strictly individual basis. This takes into account the anatomical features of the patient, the condition of the soft tissue and mammary glands and, of course, the patient’s wishes. The main goal of New Mexico mammoplasty is to create beautiful breast volumetric and saving this result for many years.

Breast augmentation is performed as follows: under the pectoralis major muscle or under the mammary gland a special implant install, filled with a special liquid. These implants are made of silicone, their use allows to lift and enlarge breasts.

It is also possible incisions along the edge pigmented part of the nipple or to the axilla – scars are less noticeable in the future. The surgeon creates a kind of “pockets” beneath the pectoralis major muscle or directly right behind the breast tissue, and placed the silicone implant. The incision is sutured cosmetic suture.

Recovery period

Breast Cosmetic Surgery In Las Cruces Wih Dr. Miguel Gallegos

You can bother the temporary painful sensation during the first 2-5 days after breast augmentation in NM. You can return to the daily rhythm of life in 3-4 days after surgery. It is also necessary to limit raise arms up above the shoulder – up to 3 weeks, in other cases – 5 days.

The active physical activity is admitted 3 weeks after surgery. Efficacy results are evaluated after 2-3 months after surgery.

As for the scars, then over time they become paler, and their final form is more dependent on the characteristics of the patient and the healing process than on the skill of the plastic surgeon.

Before the scar will take its final form, it is can take up to 2 years.
