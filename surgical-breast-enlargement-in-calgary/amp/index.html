The main objective in the planning of breast augmentation surgery is a competent, well-founded and accurate for the long term (10 years or more) the layer selection for the installation of the implant.

Options for breast implant placement:

  1. under the glandular tissue;
  2. under the fascia of the pectoralis major muscle;
  3. dual plane breast augmentation: the upper edge under the pectoralis major muscle, the lower part (large) under the glandular tissue;
  4. completely under the muscle (a muscular pocket).

Selecting an installation method depends on:

  1. breast position on the chest wall. It is may be congenitally high, medium and low;

  5. thickness, area, elasticity and anatomical features of pectoralis major muscle. We should not forget that in some people they are partially or completely absent;
