The stem cell cost of breast augmentation varies by surgeon, region of the country, and difficulty of each procedure. The average cost for a stem cell breast augmentation in the United States is about $5,000 to $10,000.

In Florida where I practice, the average price for stem cell breast augmentation is about $5,500 to $8,000.

This price includes the surgeon’s fee, anesthesia fee, and the facility fee; prices advertised for less may not include these necessary charges.

The price may be less for saline implants and more for silicone breast implants. Additional charges will apply depending on the complexity of the operation and other necessary procedures such as a breast lift or removal of previous implants.

Breast augmentation cost varies greatly depending on several factors

One of the biggest factors affecting the cost of surgery is geographic location. When looking at cost in areas like Manhattan this is not always a good means of camparison. this is one of the most expensive cities in the world, and everything costs more there. In a smaller city or suburban area the cost may be quite diffierent. If you are trying to get a good idea of the reasonable cost for an augment I would suggest that you call some of the board certified plastic surgeons in your area to get a range. When you do this make sure the cost includes everything needed to do the surgery.

In other words, make sure there are no costs that are hidden or not discussed. In general, the cost should cover the surgeon’s fee, the implant costs, anesthesia fee’s (ask who will administer the anesthesia-this can affect the cost), and the facility fee. Also ask whether the fee covers pre-and post-op visits. Also ask how the cost of complications will be addressed. Finally, if revisions of the surgery are needed how will this be charged. When inquiring about cost in your area make sure you are comparing apples to apples, and make sure of all the things that your are getting for that cost. (Phillip H. Nakano, MD, )
