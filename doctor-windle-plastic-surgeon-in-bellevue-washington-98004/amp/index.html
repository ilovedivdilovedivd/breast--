Statement: Dr. Brian Windle is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Bellevue, Washington, in the greater Seattle area. Named one of RealSelf’s 100 Plastic Surgery Influencers in America, Dr. Windle specializes in rapid recovery cosmetic surgery procedures with minimal downtime and scarring, such as 24-hour rapid recovery breast augmentation, drainless tummy tuck, and the MACS (minimal access cranial suspension) facelift.

“Plastic surgery requires not only the science of medicine and surgical technique, but an artistic eye as well,” says Dr. Windle. As the former Chief of Plastic Surgery and the Plastic Surgery Training Program at Case Western University in Cleveland, Ohio, he has an exceptionally well-trained artistic eye. Rhinoplasty is his favorite operation to perform, “because it involves great technical skill and must be individualized for each patient.”

Dr. Windle received his medical degree from the University of Calgary in Canada. After finishing his internship at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia, he completed a surgical and plastic surgery residency at the University of Western Ontario Schulich School of Medicine in London, Ontario. He then received specialized training in plastic and cosmetic surgery at the University of Alabama Birmingham, followed by facial plastic surgery and craniofacial plastic surgery training at Eastern Virginia Graduate School of Medicine in Norfolk, Virginia. He is board certified by both the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada.

Outside of caring for patients at his boutique practice, Dr. Windle contributes to professional publications such as the Annals of Plastic Surgery, Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the Journal of Reconstructive Microsurgery.

Dr. Windle has also been an Operation Smile volunteer surgeon since 1988. As a team leader, he performs free reconstructive surgery for disadvantaged children around the world and has guided 13 missions to the Philippines.
