Is it normal for my implants to still look like this 6 weeks out? What can I do?
Implant position
Jul 12, 2019
Tranaxillary implants can sit high, however, you are still in the recovery phase.  As swelling decreases and tissues relax they typically 'settle'.  However, your photos seem to show implants are high.  Follow up with your surgeon as they recommend. 
I'm 4'11" and 95 pounds, petite. I'm currently a 34A and want to be a C cup.
Breast implant size
Apr 27, 2018
I believe that for a surgeon to understand what a patient desires for their final breast size, requires a visual approach.  Cup size is abitrary and I do not think it is useful.  In my practice I use 3D imaging to simulate on each patients images the final look.  From this and the exam, I can then make an educated recommendation regarding the actually volume (cc's) and type of implant to use.
Will my breast width diameter be different after a BA?
Breast width
Apr 25, 2018
After augmentation more than likely your breast base width would increase.  Base width is an important measurement pre-operatively in selecting an implant size that is appropriate for your anatomy.
Which treatment would be better to tighten my tummy: Vaser liposuction or TT?
Abdominal contour
Apr 25, 2018
Skin tightening with devices light VASER are limited.  They certainly removed fat, but skin response is variable.  Based on your photos I believe you would likely acheive to best contour improvement with a tummy tuck.
How long should you wait after a c-section to have a mommy makeover?
Tummy tuck after c-section
Apr 25, 2018
There are no hard and fast dates .  If you are breast feeding I suggest waiting to have a consultation closer to when you will discontinue breast feeding.  At the least I suggest patients wait 4 to 6 months from delivery to have a consultation and at least 6 months to have surgery.
Do I definitely need a lift or would 300 or 350cc fill out the loose skin?
Is a lift necessary
Apr 24, 2018

Length of surgery

Do I need a face lift or inJectables/fillers?
Aging face
