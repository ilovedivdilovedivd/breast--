41, female, more than average loose skin after 50lb weight loss, will more weight loss help or tum
Should I wait?
Jun 12, 2023
You should wait until maximal wait loss has occurred. Once that occurs the extra skin and fatty tissue will likely be excised (probably vertically and horizontally) and the abdominal rectus muscles will be plicated (sutured together in the mid-line) to achieve a much tighter and flatter abdomen. Best of luck to you!
