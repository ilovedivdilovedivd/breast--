Should I remove L shape Nasal Implant and do a rib graft for my nose?
Implant in the tip usually is not good in the long run
Jul 21, 2023
It is a relatively older technique and concept to use implant at the nasal tip (ie. L shaped implant) for the problems that you mentioned regarding thinning out nasal tip and having implant extruding in the long run. How it will eventually be really depends on how thick your nasal skin is and also if the surgeon had placed some protective layers on top like on lay ear cartilages etc. But most rhinoplasty surgeon nowadays do not like any implant materials at the nasal tip.

How can I fix the bald patches? Will a hair transplant work?
Hair transplant can help
Jan 10, 2023
When the hair follicles are just atrophic or diminutive you can try treatments like topical minoxidil or PRP injection etc to revive them, but when they are completely dead then likely only hair transplant will help. the cost will depend on the area needed and how many follicles are needed. Is hair loss 5 months post hair transplant normal?
Can be normal hair shedding
Jan 10, 2023
First of all, five months after surgery you are not seeing the full result yet. Typically takes about one year to see the final result after hair transplant. Second, transplanted follicles though follow the behavior of the donor site, which usually are taken from the more testosterone resistant back of scalp area, can still fall off like any regular hair. Normal people have about 50 to 100 hair fall off on a daily basis.
Hair thinning, shedding and patchy 2 years after hair transplant, 5000 grafts

So no matter what hair transplant surgery you have done, you still need to continue certain maintenance treatment like finasteride, minoxidil, supplements, light therapy etc.
