Mammoplasty in Honolulu, HI is a cosmetic breast surgery, that giving the opportunity to change its size and shape. This is one of the most popular plastic surgery operations and most well-established: the first breast plastic surgery were performed in the 19th century.

Since then, the technique of breast surgery is constantly being improved.

Modern aesthetical surgery offers a wide variety of techniques and modifications mammoplasty. In some cases, changes in breast shape is performed by moving its own tissues, in others – with the use of the implant.

Both techniques is safe, as confirmed by many years of experience of carrying operations on breast augmentation.

Breast augmentation with implants

The implants used for breast augmentation by surgery. The surgical approach to emplacing a breast implant depends on the shape of the breast and the desired result: the first method when implants are placed immediately below the breast tissue, the second method – beneath the pectoralis major muscle.

The main task of a plastic surgeon to achieve beautiful natural shape and volume of the breast. The breast implant is emplaced through the incision. There are three types of surgical incisions inframammary or periareolar (made along the periphery of the areola), as well as through transaxillary access.

The surgeon is planning the shape of the implant, place of installation and the type an incision on the preparatory stage of breast augmentation surgery in Honolulu. The physiological characteristics of the patient and his wishes are taken into account when selecting the implant.

The choice of implants for breast augmentation surgery

The Question Of Silicone Vs. saline

The result of the operation is a complex and dynamic process that depends on the ever-changing conditions of the soft tissues that surround and support the implant.

Changes occurs due to variations hormonal background, fluctuations in body weight, playing sports, pregnancy and lactation. The implant is the only constant component of the surgery.

That is why to the implant is given so much attention. Attention. Its shape, size, surface character and manufacturer is important. Dissatisfaction with the shape and size of the breast is what motivates a woman to think about plastic surgery for breast augmentation. As a rule, every woman knows: what breast size is best for her.

Very often on her decision affects fashion trends, the recommendations of friends and wishes of the men. However, you should not neglect the advice of a plastic surgeon and discuss in detail all the nuances in the selection of the implant.

The size of breast implants is measured in cubic centimeters, the most popular are the implants from 200 to 300 cc.

The shape of implants are divided into round or teardrop-shaped (anatomical).
