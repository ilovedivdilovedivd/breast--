Hi.
The simple answer is yes it can be fixed with a tip rhinoplasty which can refine the tip and give more structural support to the tip of the nose. Having said that the main reason for a rhinoplasty is for better aesthetics or better breathing. If you dont mind the look of your nose and your breathing is fine then dont do anything and stop squishing your nose! Just fixing the squishiness is not s good indication for surgery.
