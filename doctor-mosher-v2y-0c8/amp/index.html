Statement: Dr. Mathew Mosher is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the Medical Director of YES Medspa and Cosmetic Surgery Centre in Langley, British Columbia. With over 26 years of experience, he leads an experienced team that is dedicated to performing individually optimized cosmetic surgical procedures and nonsurgical treatments, finding the best solutions for each patient to create exceptionally natural-looking results.

“Plastic surgery can be life-changing, and each patient has unique, highly individualized concerns and goals,” says Dr. Mosher. “I take the time to understand each patient’s needs, and to inform and educate them about all of their surgical and nonsurgical options for improving their body image.”

Dr. Mosher earned his medical degree from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver before going on to an internship in general surgery at Saint Paul’s Hospital in Vancouver and a residency in plastic and reconstructive surgery at the University of Saskatchewan in Saskatoon.

He then headed to Santa Ana, California, where he was chosen for a prestigious fellowship in aesthetic surgery under the tutelage of renowned plastic surgeon Dr. B.F. Connell.

Additional training in Toronto, Honolulu, and Dallas followed before he returned to Vancouver and joined YES Surgery Centre. In 1997, Dr. Mosher was one of the first physicians in B.C. to introduce laser resurfacing, laser hair removal, microdermabrasion, dermal fillers, and pharmaceutical grade skin care to his medical practice. He maintains a commitment to teaching medical students, plastic surgery residents, physicians, nurses and laser technicians, which helps keep his staff advancing and learning.

“Plastic and reconstructive surgery requires an attention to detail and a dedication to lifelong learning, unlike many other aspects of medicine,” says Dr. Mosher. “Although my focus for the past 20 years has been cosmetic surgery and cosmetic treatments, I continue to be inspired by the advances made in all areas of plastic and reconstructive surgery.”

Dr. Mosher is board-certified by the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada – Plastic Surgery. He is a member of the Canadian Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the Canadian Society of Plastic Surgeons, Canadian Laser Aesthetic Surgery Society, and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

He is also an Associate Professor at the University of British Columbia’s Division of Plastic Surgery, an Executive of the Canadian Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and a past president of the Canadian Laser Aesthetic Specialists Society.

Dr. Mosher takes pride in making time to communicate clearly with patients, help them understand their options, and guide them through the entire process, to ensure that their expectations align with their result. “We ensure that the advice we give patients is something they can rely upon and will always be offered with their best interests in mind,” he says.

As a volunteer with Project H.A.N.D.S., Dr. Mosher has traveled to Guatemala to provide plastic and reconstructive surgeries to impoverished children and adults in need. Whoever and wherever his patients are, he aims to give them results that lead to satisfaction and self-confidence.
