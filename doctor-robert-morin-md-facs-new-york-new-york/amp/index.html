What does an internal bra do?
Internal bra
Apr 16, 2020
An internal bra describes the process of reinforcing and supporting the lower portion of the breast in the vicinity of the inframammary fold by using some type of material. I commonly use either BellaDerm (acellular human dermis) or Galatea (synthetic surgical mesh) in complex revision cases where I need to reconstruct the "floor" upon which a breast implant sits.
What would you suggest for severe breast asymmetry? I have multiple questions
Breast asymmetry
Apr 16, 2020
Breast asymmetry is extremely common and there are many techniques available that allow us to improve symmetry. For minor asymmetry, differences in implant pocket dissection and the use of slightly different sized implants can be very helpful. In cases of more severe asymmetry, completely different surgical procedures can be performed on each breast. For example, one breast may require only an implant while the other breast may require both an implant and a lift.
How to reduce risk of capsular contracture before first breast augmentation?
Prevention of capsular contracture
Apr 16, 2020
Capsular contracture is a well known complication of breast augmentation surgery. While the cause of capsular contracture is not completely known, there are several things that can be done in an attempt to decrease the risk. Placement of the implant under the pectoralis muscle, meticulous surgical technique and the use of an implant funnel and the "no touch technique" are all believed to reduce the risk.

Is this double-bubble or bottom-ing out? If so, how can it be revised?
Double bubble deformity
Apr 16, 2020
The double bubble deformity occurs when an implant falls below and behind an inframammary fold that is still partially intact. Fortunately, there are several techniques available that are capable of successfully correcting this. The best course of action is to discuss surgical options with a board certified plastic surgeon.
Are my breasts too far apart after my 325cc implants a year ago?
Implants too far apart
Apr 16, 2020
Breast augmentation is a delicate balance between desire and anatomy. Desired breast size, existing nipple location and breast implant width are the three most important factors when it comes to the correct placement of the implants along the horizontal axis. These three variables should be discussed at length during the preoperative consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon.
What would you do to fix my bottoming out following two surgeries already?
Bottoming out
Apr 16, 2020
Bottoming out of breast implants is the result of a lack of support in the vicinity of the "floor" upon which the implants sit. This floor consists of the lower pole breast skin and soft tissue and the inframammary fold attachments. If the integrity of any of these structures is compromised, bottoming out can occur. I have had significant success reconstructing the lower pole of the breast and the inframammary fold using both Alloderm and Galatea.
Can my fat gut be removed and what surgery would you recommend?
Removal of belly fat
Apr 15, 2020
There are many different ways to remove "belly fat." The first is diet and exercise, the second is liposuction, the third is a tummy tuck and in the case of intra abdominal fat, the fourth is diet modification and tightening of the anterior abdominal wall. In order to determine exactly which procedure is best for a patient, an in person consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon is required.
How badly do I need a tummy tuck to reduce a rectangle look? Is just lipo an option?
Tummy tuck vs. lipo
Apr 15, 2020
Abdominal liposuction is a great procedure when there is either minimal or no extra skin. When extra skin is present, the best result is usually obtained by performing a tummy tuck. Liposuction of the flanks is almost always performed at the same time as a tummy tuck in order to achieve an hourglass appearance instead of a boxy or rectangular appearance.
Am I a good candidate for a mommy makeover?
Mommy makeover
Apr 15, 2020
The best candidate for a mommy makeover is someone who is done having children, is at a stable weight, is a non smoker and is unhappy with the appearance of her abdomen and breasts. Generally an abdominoplasty with liposuction is performed, in addition to either a breast augmentation and/or a breast lift. The specific details of any procedure can be determined based on one's specific anatomy and desires while in consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon.
