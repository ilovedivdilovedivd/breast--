Can a surgeon please explain the facility fee to me?
Facility Fee
Nov 17, 2023
Surgical Facilities are very expensive to build and to staff. As such, there is a separate fee to use them. Some facilities may be owned by the surgeon, and some are not, but either way there needs to be a fee to cover the cost of using the surgical facility.

Quintessa

My biggest complaint: sagging jowls and marionette lines. Should I get HALO or BBL?
BBL or Halo
Sep 22, 2023
A BBL will target the abnormal reds and browns of the skin and help preserve the overall quality of the skin. A Halo targets the collagen in the dermis and helps build more new collagen. This causes an improvement in the texture of the skin and some skin "tightening". However, I doubt a Halo will improve your jowls or marionettes much. I would recommend you consider some filler or for even more improvement, a minilift.

Andrew Campbell, M.D.
Facial Rejuvenation Specialist

Halo Laser and Broadband Light Laser - are these lasers safe for Asian skin?
Halo and BBL are safe
Aug 14, 2023
Both Halo and BBL, when performed by experienced providers, are safe for Asian skin. Depending on the severity of your skin issues, the improvement can be quite dramatic. Patients that have less dramatic problems will have less dramatic results, but both promote healthier younger looking skin.

Andrew Campbell, M.D.
Facial Rejuvenation Specialist

Can a lower eye skin pinch be revised and how soon after a procedure?
Be patient
Jan 16, 2023
I would recommend that you give this time. Lower eyelids can take at least 2 months, and sometimes 6 months or even longer to completely normalize. The mild swelling in the tissue makes the skin wrinkle more than it would when the swelling is gone. Also, your incisions look like they are exactly where they should be and once they completely heal, they will not be very noticable.

Andrew Campbell, M.D.
Facial Rejuvenation Specialist
Quintessa
