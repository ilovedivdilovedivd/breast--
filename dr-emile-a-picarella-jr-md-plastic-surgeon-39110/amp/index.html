I want to remove 700cc implants then get a lift, my surgeon won’t do it all at once. What w
Second opinion
Feb 11, 2021
Have you attempted to get another opinion? Implant removal with mastopexy are performed simultaneously commonly. While there is nothing wrong with a staged approach, it isn’t necessary. But please keep in mind that revision rates are higher when performed simultaneously. Hope this helps.
I really want to get implants but I don’t want a lift, is it possible for me?
Lift required
Feb 11, 2021
Larger implants can often abate the need for a mastopexy, but at your grade of ptosis the mastopexy is required.
What does your HBA1C need to be to get a breast augmentation? I’m Type 1 diabetic.
Too high
Feb 11, 2021
In my practice HbA1C has to be very near 7.0 prior to proceeding with any cosmetic surgery since hyperglycemia is a risk factor for wound healing delays and infection. Either one would be a disaster for a BA.
Should I get breast implants or a breast lift?
Implants
Feb 11, 2021
Hello. I recommend implants through your prior areola reduction incisions in order to add upper pole volume and enhance your lower poles. Good luck!
How can I decide between 250 or 275cc?
No worries
Feb 11, 2021
At 66 inches, I would recommend 275 because it is the larger of the two you listed. This volume will not look fake like the old Hollywood style. Honestly, there is such a small volume difference that you would not make a mistake with either choice. Relax and enjoy your new look.
