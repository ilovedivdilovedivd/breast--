Lower facelift and neck lift
Jun 12, 2023
From the photos, I would recommend lower facelift ( will correct the jowls ) and lift up the cheek (as long as it is a deep plane facelift ). I also would recommend a neck lift to make sure your lower face and neck are balanced and the transition is smooth and youthful.
Which is better – incision in the hairline or just below the sideburn for lower facelift?
Depends on the skin laxity and the planned amount of skin to be removed
Jun 9, 2023
I use both incisions. If I anticipate a larger amount of skin to be removed I will use the sideburn to avoid moving it (not a good look) but if the amount of the skin is less I do the hairline incision.
