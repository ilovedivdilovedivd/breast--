Statement: Dr. Ellen A. Mahony is a leading plastic surgeon with over 30 years of experience. Located in Westport, Connecticut, her boutique plastic surgery practice provides patients with personalized, high-quality surgical and nonsurgical rejuvenation procedures.

“With a decision like cosmetic surgery, a skilled board-certified plastic surgeon is a must,” says Dr. Mahony. “She needs to understand exactly who you are, what you want, and why. In me, you’ll have more than a physician. You’ll have a confidant, personal support, and the comfort of being in the right hands.”

Dr. Mahony received her bachelor’s degree in physical therapy from the University of Connecticut in 1979 and went on to earn her master’s degree in international health care from Yale University Graduate School in New Haven, Connecticut. She then traveled to Africa to engage in research with the Hanang Village Health Project in Tanzania, which later became the platform for the country’s nationalized healthcare program.

After returning to the United States, Dr. Mahony attended medical school at the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, becoming one of a select few female physicians invited to enter the university’s general surgery residency. She went on to complete a hand and microsurgery fellowship as well as the prestigious plastic and reconstructive surgery residency.

A RealSelf 100 honoree, Dr. Mahony has also been granted research, merit scholarships, and several awards from professional medical organizations, including the American Medical Association.

Dr. Mahony is one of the few female plastic surgeons in Connecticut to be board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. She is also a member of the American College of Surgeons and the American Society for Aesthetic Surgery.
