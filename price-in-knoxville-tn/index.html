The relative contraindications for breast augmentation.
  1. Autoimmune diseases such as systemic lupus erythematosus, and scleroderma.
  2. Conditions that impede the normal wound healing, such as diabetes and disorders in blood coagulation system.
  3. Reduced immunnitet. Breast augmentation surgery is not recomended in cases of recently receiving immunosuppressive drugs.
  4. Violation of the blood supply to the breast tissue.
