Can CoolSculpting help my abdomen that also has visceral fat?
Does CoolSculpting treat visceral fat
Oct 14, 2020
Hi, great question.
CoolSculpting targets subcutaneous fat, that's the pinchable fat between your skin and the muscle.
Visceral fat is under the abdominal muscle around your internal organs. Only diet and exercise can help reduce visceral fat.

Is it okay to use Picoway Resolve on one specific sunspot on my upper cheek versus my whole face
Pico Way Resolve Fractional Laser for brown spots
Aug 24, 2020
Hi,
Great question. Depending on your skin type on the Fitzpatrick Scale from I - VI you may be a good candidate to treat that spot with Pico Way. A better choice would be to use the Zoom handpiece to spot treat and pick the appropriate and safe wavelength based on your skin type. Warmer skin types may be safe to use the nD:YAG 1064nm wavelength on the Pico Way, while fair skinned patients can use the 532nm wavelength which specific targets pigment.
Hope that helps!
