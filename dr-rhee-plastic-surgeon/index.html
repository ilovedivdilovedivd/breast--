Can I avoid a breast lift? Looking for a natural shape (Photo)

Given your desire to be a D cup, a larger implant may be used, but it’s likely you will still require some skin tightening. The nature of of the lift will depend on your expectations and an in person exam, but going without it may result in tissue sagging below your implant.
