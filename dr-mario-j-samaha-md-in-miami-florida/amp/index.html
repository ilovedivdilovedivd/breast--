Is it too late to change my implant size?
Never too late
Jul 28, 2023
Hello – thank you for your question. The only time it’s too late is after surgery! So make sure you ask your plastic surgeon and communicate your wishes prior to surgery. We usually carry multiple size implants in our office/hospital, so it should not be an issue! Hope this helps!
How long after being off Accutane can you get a breast augmentation under the muscle?
6 months
Jul 28, 2023
Hello and thank you for your question. It usually is recommend to wait at least 6 months from your last dose to perform elective surgery, as it could impact scaring and healing. Each plastic surgeon is different, so make sure to ask your plastic surgeon about his pre-op requirement. A clearance from your dermatologist could be needed. Hope this helps! Make sure to ask your plastic surgeon all your questions during your consultation!
Does placing the implants under the muscle reduce its size? How much?
Should not be different
Jul 28, 2023
Hello – thank you for your question. The choice of pocket (sub glandular vs submusclar) shouldn’t really affect the “size” of the implant, but more so its look. Under the muscle usually give a more natural look with a more “slopy” transition between your chest to breast. Both have their pros and cons, so make sure to ask your plastic surgeon about the difference! Hope this helps!
Right breast not dropping. 6 months post-op
Best is to ask your plastic surgeon
Jul 28, 2023
Hello,
Thank you for your question. The speed the implants drop depends on multiple factors, and is different from person to person. It is best to ask your plastic surgeon, and or to visit him for an examination.
Is there much difference between 275cc and 295cc?
Not much
Jul 28, 2023
Hello,
There is not much difference between both from a weight and measurement perspective. The implants will weight the same once place in your breast, yes.
Hope this helps! Make sure to ask your plastic surgeon all your question during your consultation!
Can I avoid a breast lift with implants alone?
Can make it happen
Jul 28, 2023
Hello,
Thank you for your question. It can work, yes. As long as you are ok with some asymmetry after surgery, especially with the areola size/location. The breast size difference can be reduced by choosing different size/profile implants if needed. Make sure to ask your plastic surgeon all your questions during your consultation!
Hope this helps!
Implants without going much larger?
Depends on anatomy
Jul 28, 2023
Hello,
Thank you for your question. It is possible, however it will depend on how much ptosis (or saggyness) you have, the amount of skin, and the look you want. It is best to schedule an appointment for a consultation where your plastic surgeon can examine you, listen to what you wish for and come up with a plan that best works for you. Make sure to ask all your questions during your consultation!
