9 months after tummy tuck - Swelling and tightness still present. What can be causing this? (phot
Tummy Tuck - still swollen
Dec 27, 2022
Swelling can persist up to a year or more after surgery. A tummy tuck usually includes muscle tightening and some of the tightness you are feeling could be continued healing from the muscle tightening. I would advise continued follow up with your surgeon and if you are really concerned, you can seek another opinion by a qualified plastic surgeon in your area.

How long after a tummy tuck can you travel by air plane back home?
Tummy Tuck - amount of time needed before flying
Dec 27, 2022
The most important thing after having surgery is to maintain a good relationship with your surgeon. Unfortunately, sometimes complications can happen, both minor and large and not having access to your surgeon in the event of an issue can be life threatening.
Before having surgery in another state/country, make sure you discuss a clear plan with your surgeon in the event that something goes wrong and where you should go for wound/drain care.
Best of luck!

I'm 8 months postpartum - mini tummy tuck or full?
Tummy Tuck - mini or full?
Dec 27, 2022
Hi!
The decision between a mini tummy tuck and a full really depends on your physical exam in evaluating how much extra skin you have as well as the separation in your muscles. A full tummy tuck will change the position of your belly button and will allow for more removal of the loose skin and striae (stretch marks) than would be possible with a mini. Hope this helps!
