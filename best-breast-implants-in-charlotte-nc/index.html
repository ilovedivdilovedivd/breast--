Breast augmentation in Charlotte NC is an operation that relates to reconstructive and aesthetic surgery. The operation can be performed both on the patient’s request, and for medical reasons (if the breast was incorrectly formed or breast reconstruction after amputation).

History of breast augmentation

For the first time such an operation with the use of liquid paraffin injections were performed in 1889, and attempts to use for the implantation of various substances found its place the beginning of the 19th century.

In the role of the first implants was used ivory, glass spherical shape, artificial plastics, animal cartilage, and adipose tissues, polystyrene, polyethylene, polyurethane, and other implants made of synthetic and animals materials. Implantation of animal fat did not lead to the desired result, as it is rapidly absorbed by the body, and the use of other materials lead to entering of an infection, the appearance of tumors and other negative consequences.

Surgery Breast Augmentation To Correct A Reduction In Breast Volume After Pregnancy

Injections of liquid paraffin were made for the girls of easy virtue to increase breast volume in the fortieth years, but such risky actions rarely lead to the desired result, and often ended of solidification of of breast and mastectomy (complete removal of the breast).

In the sixties, after more than fifty thousand unsuccessful operations, the first prototypes of the modern silicone implants have been developed by surgeons F.Dzherou T.Kroninym from by Houston.

In the early eighties there were the first reports about the dangers of silicone implants, which is why they were banned for general use (except for medical purposes). They were replaced by implants with saline until 2006. However, throughout this long period of time did not reveal evidence of harm and safety of implants.

At the moment, for the breast job in Charlotte NC are often used fifth generation implants of round or tapered that allows to achieve perfect anatomical shape. They also have a textured surface to reduce the likelihood rotational and displacement after the operation.

Scar After Breast Implants

The safety analysis of such implants is still at a preliminary stage in the US, but they is very popular in almost all countries of the world. These implants are sufficiently dense, have shape memory, and the gel does not flow through the shell and has a high degree cohesion. Rupture of implants and the occurrence of capsular contracture is less common, comparing with previous generations of implants.

The implant can be installed directly under the breast tissue or under the pectoralis major muscle. In my practice, I try to choose a position of the implant, so that it was sufficiently closed of the tissue and maximally invisible.

I recommend installation of implant under the muscle for slender female patients. As regards the of volumes, it is theoretically possible to install implant of any size. But we do aesthetic surgery, so in order to achieve a beautiful result the volume of the implant must be proportionate.
