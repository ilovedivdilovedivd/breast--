Is it possible if I do vein Sclerotherapy, that I will get blood clots again?
Blood Clots and sclerotherapy
Feb 11, 2019
I will not do any vein treatment for unto two years after a Blood clot in the legs (DVT) or a clot that goes to the lungs (PE). Most patients are on blood thinners for 3-6 months. I do sclerotherapy and vein treatments on such patients after assessing their deep veins to make sure there is no residual blockage. I will also put such patients on oral anticoagulants couple of days before to a month after treatment.
