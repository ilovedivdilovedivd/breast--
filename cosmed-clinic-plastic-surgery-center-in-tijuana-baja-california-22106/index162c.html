Case analysis
Sep 9, 2023
Hi, It's hard to analyze skin looseness by seeing a picture, but in case you have loose skin, there are multiple radiofrequency devices that can help you tight skin, this can be performed as a ambulatory procedure with topical anesthesia. In case you have severe skin laxity then a surgical procedure will be your best option. Hope this is helpful.

Hi, after analyzing your pictures I can say that you have a nice body shape, with minimal excess of skin (based on the pictures). Ask your plastic surgeon if he offers this treatment, or in case he doesn't have it what are his recommendations to tight the skin in your abdomen....
