Is it safe to get the Forma treatment while on tretinoin cream?
Forma and Tretinoin
May 11, 2022
Thank you for your question! There are no contraindications for using tretinoin while receiving Forma treatments. However, we would recommend that you not use your tretinoin the night before your treatment, the day of your treatment, or the following day. This will minimize any irritation. We also recommend that you speak to your provider. Best of luck to you!

N95 Masks and Microneedling
May 11, 2022
